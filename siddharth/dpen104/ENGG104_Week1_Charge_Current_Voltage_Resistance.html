Practice 21

A 14 V source feeds series resistors 2 ohm and 6 ohm. Find current and voltage across each resistor.

Solution

I=14/(2+6)=1.750 A. V1=IR1=3.500 V and V2=IR2=10.500 V.

Practice 22

Current is 0.20 A for 5 minutes at 9 V. Find charge moved and energy delivered.

Solution

Q=It=0.20*300=60.0 C. E=VQ=9*60.0=540.0 J.

Practice 23

A resistor has conductance 0.0030 S. If 16 V is applied, find current and resistance.

Solution

I=VG=16*0.0030=0.0480 A. R=1/G=333.3 ohm.

Practice 24

Two resistors R and 2R in series across 24 V draw 1 A. Find R and both drops.

Solution

R_total=V/I=24.00 ohm=3R so R=8.00 ohm. Drops: IR=8.00 V and I(2R)=16.00 V.
