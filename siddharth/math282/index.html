MATH282 / ADVANCED ENGINEERING MATH

Advanced Engineering Mathematics

Detailed topic-by-topic lessons based mainly on Glyn James style: motivation, method scaffold, worked examples, practice sets, and mini tests.

Lesson 1 — Multivariable Functions & Partial Derivatives
Functions of several variables, geometric interpretation, chain rule, implicit partial derivatives, and local approximation.
Weeks 1-2 focus
Lesson 2 — Double Integrals, Order Switching, Polar Coordinates
Set up and evaluate double integrals, reverse integration order, area/volume, and polar-coordinate transforms.
Weeks 2-3 focus
Lesson 3 — Laplace Transforms for ODEs
Core transform table, initial-value problems, shift theorems, Heaviside and delta functions, and transform techniques.
Weeks 3-5 focus
Lesson 4 — Fourier Series + Beta/Error Functions
Convergence ideas, sine/cosine/general interval expansions, and special functions used in engineering models.
Weeks 6-7 focus
Lesson 5 — Probability, Random Variables & Distributions
Probability rules, diagrams, summary statistics, discrete/continuous distributions, and CLT-linked foundations.
Weeks 8-10 focus
Lesson 6 — Statistical Inference, Regression, ANOVA
Confidence intervals, hypothesis testing, t/F tests, correlation, least-squares regression, one-way ANOVA, and nonparametric methods.
Weeks 11-13 focus
