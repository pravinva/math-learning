Lesson 4 - Fourier Series, Beta Function, Error Function

This lesson connects periodic decomposition (Fourier) with special integrals used in probability and diffusion engineering models.

1) Motivation

Vibration and signal models are naturally periodic; Fourier converts complicated periodic shapes into sine/cosine building blocks. Beta and error functions appear when standard antiderivatives do not exist in elementary form.

2) Method Scaffold

Fourier series on \((-L,L)\):

\[ f(x)\sim \frac{a_0}{2}+\sum_{n=1}^\infty\left(a_n\cos\frac{n\pi x}{L}+b_n\sin\frac{n\pi x}{L}\right) \]

  1. Check parity: even \(\Rightarrow b_n=0\), odd \(\Rightarrow a_0=a_n=0\).
  2. Compute required coefficients only.
  3. Use interval and period carefully.

3) Solved Examples

Example 1 — Fourier coefficients

\(f(x)=x\) on \((-\pi,\pi)\).

  1. \(f\) is odd \(\Rightarrow a_0=a_n=0\).
  2. \(b_n=\frac{1}{\pi}\int_{-\pi}^{\pi}x\sin(nx)\,dx=\frac{2}{\pi}\int_0^\pi x\sin(nx)\,dx\).
  3. After integration by parts: \(b_n=2(-1)^{n+1}/n\).
  4. \(x\sim2\sum_{n=1}^\infty (-1)^{n+1}\frac{\sin(nx)}{n}\).

Example 2 — Half-range sine series

\(f(x)=x,\ 0

  1. \(b_n=\frac{2}{\pi}\int_0^\pi x\sin(nx)\,dx\).
  2. \(b_n=2(-1)^{n+1}/n\), same form as above.

Example 3 — Beta function identity

\(B(p,q)=\int_0^1 t^{p-1}(1-t)^{q-1}dt = \frac{\Gamma(p)\Gamma(q)}{\Gamma(p+q)}\).

Use to evaluate \( \int_0^1 \sqrt{t}(1-t)^2 dt = B(3/2,3)\).

  1. \(\Gamma(3)=2!,\ \Gamma(3/2)=\frac{\sqrt{\pi}}{2},\ \Gamma(9/2)=\frac{105\sqrt{\pi}}{16}\).
  2. Integral \(= \frac{\Gamma(3/2)\Gamma(3)}{\Gamma(9/2)}=\frac{16}{105}\).

Example 4 — Error function

\[ \operatorname{erf}(x)=\frac{2}{\sqrt{\pi}}\int_0^x e^{-u^2}du \]

Evaluate \(\int_0^a e^{-u^2}du = \frac{\sqrt{\pi}}{2}\operatorname{erf}(a)\).

4) Practice Set

  1. Find Fourier series of constant \(f(x)=1\) on \((-\pi,\pi)\).
  2. Find parity and non-zero coefficient family for \(f(x)=x^2\).
  3. Find first three non-zero terms for sine series of \(f(x)=x\) on \((0,\pi)\).
  4. Find first three cosine-series terms for \(f(x)=x\) on \((0,\pi)\).
  5. Use Beta/Gamma identity to evaluate \(B(2,3)\).
  6. Evaluate \(\int_0^1 t(1-t)^3dt\).
  7. Express \(\int_0^2 e^{-u^2}du\) with \(\operatorname{erf}\).
  8. State convergence value of Fourier series at jump discontinuity.
  9. For \(f(x)=|x|\) on \((-\pi,\pi)\), which coefficients vanish?
  10. Explain one engineering use of Fourier series.
