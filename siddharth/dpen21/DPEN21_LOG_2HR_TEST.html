Section A — Logarithms, Exponentials, and Function Basics

15 marks
Q1. Evaluate: \( \log_3 27 \), \( \log_2 128 \), \( \log_4 0.25 \). (3)
Q2. Simplify:
  • \( \log_2 16 + \log_2 8 \)
  • \( \log 2 + \log 5 \)
  • \( \log_2 18 - 2\log_2 3 \)
(3)
Q3. Solve for \(x\): \( \log_2 16 + \log_2 x = \log_2 32 \), and \( e^x = 25 \). (3)
Q4. If \(f(x)=x^3-x\) and \(g(x)=x^2+1\):
  • state whether \(f\) is odd, even, or neither;
  • state whether \(g\) is odd, even, or neither;
  • find \( (f\circ g)(x) \).
(4)
Q5. For \(h(x)=|x-2|-3\), state:
  • vertex coordinates,
  • x-intercepts,
  • range.
(2)

Section B — Graphing (All Key Curves)

20 marks
Q6. Straight line: Find equation, gradient, and both intercepts of the line through \((1,2)\), \((-1,3)\). (4)
Q7. Circle: Sketch \( (x-2)^2+(y+1)^2=9 \). Label centre, radius, and intercepts. (3)
Q8. Parabola: Sketch \( y=(x-1)^2-4 \). Label vertex, axis of symmetry, and intercepts. (3)
Q9. Rectangular hyperbola: Sketch \( y=2+\frac{4}{x-3} \). Label centre, asymptotes, and intercepts. (4)
Q10. Exponential: Sketch \( y=2^{x-1}-3 \). State domain, range, asymptote, and intercepts. (3)
Q11. Logarithmic: Sketch \( y=\log_3(x+2)+1 \). State domain, range, asymptote, and intercepts. (3)

Section C — Inequalities and Mixed Function Applications

15 marks
Q12. Solve and represent on a number line:
  • \(2x-5 \le 9\)
  • \(-3 < 2x+1 \le 7\)
(4)
Q13. Solve the absolute value inequality \( |2x-3| \ge 5 \). (3)
Q14. Solve \( \log x - \log(x-1)=1 \), including domain check. (3)
Q15. Solve \( 2^{x+1}=4^{3x-2} \). (2)
Q16. Let \(f(x)=|x|\) and \(g(x)=x-2\). Find \( (f\circ g)(x) \), and state where it equals 0. (3)
