DPEN21 Class Skill Mastery Test 2A

Built from Week class-question styles.
12 questions. Suggested time: 50 minutes.
  1. Solve: $11x+17=39$.
  2. Solve: $\dfrac{3x-1}{5}=\dfrac{x+4}{2}$.
  3. Solve: $x^2-13x+40=0$.
  4. Solve by substitution $u=x^2$: $x^4-10x^2+9=0$.
  5. Solve: $|2x-3|=9$.
  6. Solve in interval notation: $-1<3x+2\le 8$.
  7. Find gradient and y-intercept of $3x-2y+6=0$.
  8. Find equation of line through $(2,3)$ with gradient $-4$.
  9. Find intersection of $2x+y=8$ and $x-y=1$.
  10. Given $f(x)=x^2-4x+2$, find $f(3)$ and $f(-1)$.
  11. Given $g(x)=\dfrac{1}{x-2}$, state domain and range.
  12. For $f(x)=\begin{cases}2x+1,&x\ge2\\9-2x,&x<2\end{cases}$ find $f(0),f(2),f(4)$.
