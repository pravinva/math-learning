DPEN21 Class Final Test 2 (No Hyperbola)

Weeks 4-5-6 style mixed paper.
12 questions. Suggested time: 2 hours. Hyperbola excluded.
  1. Find line parallel to $3x-y+2=0$ through $(-1,4)$.
  2. Solve simultaneous equations: $4x+y=11$, $2x-3y=-1$.
  3. Find minimum value of $y=x^2-6x+10$.
  4. Find equation of circle with centre $(3,-2)$ and radius $5$.
  5. State domain and range of $y=|x+2|-3$.
  6. Given $f(x)=\frac1x$, $g(x)=x+4$, find $f(g(x))$.
  7. Solve $\log_2 x=5$.
  8. Solve $\log_{10}(x+1)+\log_{10}(x-1)=1$.
  9. Simplify: $\ln e^{3x}-2\ln e^x$.
  10. Solve $5^{x-2}=25^{-1}$.
  11. Solve $2e^{x}-7=0$ (3 d.p.).
  12. Sketch $y=\ln(x-2)+1$ with asymptote, intercepts, domain and range.
