Skill Mastery 2 Practice Test C — Worked Answers

  1. \(3a^{4-(-2)}=3a^6\).
  2. \(x^{-2-1}y^{3-(-1)}=x^{-3}y^4=\frac{y^4}{x^3}\).
  3. \(x^{5/2-1/2}=x^2\).
  4. \(5y-1+2(y+2)=24\Rightarrow 7y+3=24\Rightarrow y=3\).
  5. \((x-5)(x-2)=0\Rightarrow x=5,2\).
  6. \(x^2+6x=-2\Rightarrow (x+3)^2=7\Rightarrow x=-3\pm\sqrt{7}\).
  7. Let \(u=x^2-3x\). \(u^2-4u-12=0\Rightarrow (u-6)(u+2)=0\). So \(x^2-3x=6\) or \(-2\). Gives \(x=\frac{3\pm\sqrt{33}}{2}\) and \(x=1,2\).
  8. \(5^{x-2}=5^2\Rightarrow x=4\).
  9. \(\frac3x=2+\frac1y=\frac{2y+1}{y}\Rightarrow x=\frac{3y}{2y+1}\).
  10. \(-8\le 4x<8\Rightarrow -2\le x<2\).
  11. \(x+5\ge4\) or \(x+5\le-4\Rightarrow x\ge-1\) or \(x\le-9\).
  12. \(3x-2=10\) or \(3x-2=-10\Rightarrow x=4\) or \(x=-\frac{8}{3}\).
