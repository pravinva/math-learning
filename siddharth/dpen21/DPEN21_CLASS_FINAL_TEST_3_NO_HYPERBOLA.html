DPEN21 Class Final Test 3 (No Hyperbola)

Weeks 4-5-6 style mixed paper.
12 questions. Suggested time: 2 hours. Hyperbola excluded.
  1. Find equation of line with x-intercept 3 and y-intercept -6.
  2. Find point of intersection of $y=2x+1$ and $y=-3x+11$.
  3. Find axis of symmetry and vertex for $y=-2x^2+8x-3$.
  4. Find centre/radius of $(x+1)^2+(y-4)^2=16$.
  5. State domain and range of $y=\dfrac{1}{x-3}$.
  6. Given $f(x)=x^2-4$, find $f^{-1}(5)$ if $x\ge0$.
  7. Solve $\log_3(2x-1)=2$.
  8. Solve $\ln x+\ln(x-3)=2$.
  9. Simplify: $\log_a(a^4)-\tfrac12\log_a(a^6)$.
  10. Solve $10^{x+1}=0.01$.
  11. Solve $e^{2x}=15$ (3 d.p.).
  12. Sketch $y=3-2e^{-x}$ showing horizontal asymptote and intercepts.
