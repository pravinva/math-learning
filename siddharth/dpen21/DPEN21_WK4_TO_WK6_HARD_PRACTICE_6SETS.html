DPEN21 Skill Mastery Prep (Weeks 4-6) - Hard Practice, 6 Sets

Six harder sets (20 questions each) across Week 4 (line geometry), Week 5 (functions/parabolas/circle/hyperbola), and Week 6 (exponential-log). Includes Strand 1 style questions (especially Q14, Q18, Q21-Q23 patterns).
Total: 120 questions. Recommended: 70-80 minutes per set. Method: attempt timed first, then mark using answer keys.

Answer Key - Set A

  1. \(y=-\frac12x+\frac52\), gradient \(-\frac12\), intercepts \((5,0),(0,\frac52)\)
  2. \(k=4\)
  3. Perpendicular (\(m_1=-\frac23,\ m_2=\frac32\), product \(-1\))
  4. \(y=-\frac34x+6\), so \(a=8,\ b=6\)
  5. \((\frac{29}{5},-3)\)
  6. \(x=3\pm2\sqrt3\), points \((3\pm2\sqrt3,\ 3\pm4\sqrt3)\)
  7. Vertex \((3,-4)\), axis \(x=3\), x-int \(1,5\), y-int \(5\), range \([-4,\infty)\)
  8. \((-\infty,-\frac12]\cup[4,\infty)\)
  9. Vertex \((\frac52,-3)\), x-int \(1,4\), y-int \(2\), range \([-3,\infty)\)
  10. Centre \((3,-4)\), radius \(4\), intersects x-axis: yes
  11. \((x-2)(y-1)=3\); centre \((2,1)\), asymptotes \(x=2,y=1\), domain \(x\neq2\), range \(y\neq1\)
  12. \(x=\frac{-1\pm\sqrt{51}}{5}\), valid (\(x\neq-\frac43,-1\))
  13. \(f^{-1}(x)=4+\frac1x\); domains \(x\neq4,\ x\neq0\)
  14. \(x=\frac12\ln15\)
  15. \(x=\frac{1+\log 7}{3}\)
  16. \(x=\frac{-(2\ln5+\ln2)}{\ln2-\ln5}\)
  17. Domain \(\mathbb{R}\), range \((-4,\infty)\), asymptote \(y=-4\)
  18. Domain \((-2,\infty)\), range \(\mathbb{R}\), asymptote \(x=-2\)
  19. \(y=e^{-2x}\)
  20. \(x=\sqrt{11}\)
