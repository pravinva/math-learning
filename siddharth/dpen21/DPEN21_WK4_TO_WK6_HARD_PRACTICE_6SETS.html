DPEN21 Skill Mastery Prep (Weeks 4-6) - Hard Practice, 6 Sets

Six harder sets (20 questions each) across Week 4 (line geometry), Week 5 (functions/parabolas/circle/hyperbola), and Week 6 (exponential-log). Includes Strand 1 style questions (especially Q14, Q18, Q21-Q23 patterns).
Total: 120 questions. Recommended: 70-80 minutes per set. Method: attempt timed first, then mark using answer keys.
Included topic focus: parabola, circle, absolute value graphs/inequalities, and logarithmic-exponential equations (embedded in every set, plus dedicated graphing/sketching booster blocks below).
