Time: 40 minutes | 12 MCQs | 2 marks each
Worked answers (separate): open answer file.

Questions

  1. [Expansion] Simplify \(2x^2+3(x-5)-5(x^2+1)\).
  2. [Expansion] Expand and simplify \(7a^2-3(a-4b)^2\).
  3. [Pascal] Sum of coefficients in \((a-2b)^5\).
  4. [Pascal] Expand \((2x+3)^4\).
  5. [Factorising] Expand then factorise \(4(3a^2-2)-24a+8\).
  6. [Factorising by grouping] Factorise \(5ac-10ad+3bc-6bd\).
  7. [Difference of squares] Factorise \((6x+z)^2-(x-4z)^2\).
  8. [Quadratic] Factorise completely \(4x^2-12x-7\).
  9. [Algebraic fractions] Simplify \(\frac{3x^3-27x}{x^2-9}\).
  10. [Fractions ops] Simplify \(\frac{5}{x-1}-\frac{2}{x+4}\).
  11. [Surds] Simplify \(3\sqrt{50}+2\sqrt8-\sqrt{18}\).
  12. [Rationalise] Rationalise \(\frac{2}{5-\sqrt6}\).
