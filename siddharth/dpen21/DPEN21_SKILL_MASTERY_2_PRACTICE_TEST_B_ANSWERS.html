Skill Mastery 2 Practice Test B — Worked Answers

  1. \(\frac{4x^6}{4x}=x^5\).
  2. \(a^{-2-(-5)}b^{-3-1}=a^3b^{-4}=\frac{a^3}{b^4}\).
  3. \(x^{4/3}x^{2/3}=x^2\).
  4. \(2(2y-5)+3(y+4)=6\Rightarrow4y-10+3y+12=6\Rightarrow7y=4\Rightarrow y=\frac47\).
  5. \(2x^2-3x-2=(2x+1)(x-2)=0\Rightarrow x=2,-\frac12\).
  6. \(x^2-8x=-7\Rightarrow (x-4)^2=9\Rightarrow x=7,1\).
  7. Let \(u=x^2+2x\). \(u^2-5u-6=0\Rightarrow (u-6)(u+1)=0\). So \(x^2+2x=6\) or \(-1\). Gives \(x=-1\pm\sqrt7\) and \(x=-1\).
  8. \(3^{x-1}=3^3\Rightarrow x=4\).
  9. \(\frac2x=3-\frac1y=\frac{3y-1}{y}\Rightarrow x=\frac{2y}{3y-1}\).
  10. \(-3\le3x<9\Rightarrow -1\le x<3\).
  11. \(2x-1>5\) or \(2x-1<-5\Rightarrow x>3\) or \(x<-2\).
  12. Need RHS nonnegative: \(2x+1\ge0\Rightarrow x\ge-\frac12\). Cases give \(x=\frac23\) valid and \(x=-4\) invalid (RHS negative). So solution \(x=\frac23\).
