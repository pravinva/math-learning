DPEN21 Skill Mastery 1 Practice Test D

Time: 40 minutes | 12 MCQs | 2 marks each
Worked answers (separate): open answer file.

Questions

  1. [Expansion] Simplify \(6x^2-4(x-3)+2(x^2-5)\).
  2. [Expansion] Expand and simplify \(8a^2-5(a-2b)^2\).
  3. [Pascal] Sum of coefficients in \((3a+b)^4\).
  4. [Pascal] Expand \((x-2)^5\).
  5. [Factorising] Expand then factorise \(5(2a^2-3)-20a+15\).
  6. [Factorising by grouping] Factorise \(12ac-8ad+9bc-6bd\).
  7. [Difference of squares] Factorise \((3x+4y)^2-(x-y)^2\).
  8. [Quadratic] Factorise completely \(5x^2+13x-6\).
  9. [Algebraic fractions] Simplify \(\frac{x^3-16x}{x^2-16}\).
  10. [Fractions ops] Simplify \(\frac{2}{x+3}+\frac{7}{x-1}\).
  11. [Surds] Simplify \(2\sqrt{45}-\sqrt{20}+3\sqrt{5}\).
  12. [Rationalise] Rationalise \(\frac{4}{3+\sqrt{7}}\).
