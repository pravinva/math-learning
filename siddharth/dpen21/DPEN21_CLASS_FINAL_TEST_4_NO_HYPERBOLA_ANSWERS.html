DPEN21 CLASS FINAL TEST 4 NO HYPERBOLA — Worked Solutions

Full worked solutions (separate answer key).
  1. Given slope $\frac12$, perpendicular slope is $-2$. Through $(4,5)$: $y-5=-2(x-4)\Rightarrow y=-2x+13$.
  2. From $x-2y=1$, $x=1+2y$. Substitute into $3x+y=14$: $3+6y+y=14\Rightarrow y=\frac{11}{7}$, $x=\frac{29}{7}$.
  3. $x^2-7x+10=(x-5)(x-2)=0$, roots $x=2,5$. Upward parabola: negative between roots, positive outside.
  4. Circle equation: $(x+2)^2+(y-1)^2=16$.
  5. $y=\sqrt{x+3}-2$: domain $x\ge-3$, range $y\ge-2$.
  6. $g(f(x))=(2x-1)^2+3=4x^2-4x+4$.
  7. $\log_4(x+3)=2\Rightarrow x+3=16\Rightarrow x=13$.
  8. $\log x=\log4+\log5=\log20\Rightarrow x=20$.
  9. $\ln(3x)-\ln x=\ln\frac{3x}{x}=\ln3$ (valid for $x>0$).
  10. $9^x=(3^2)^x=3^{2x}$ and RHS $3^{2x-1}$. Equality would require $2x=2x-1$, impossible. So no solution.
  11. $e^{x-2}=6\Rightarrow x-2=\ln6\Rightarrow x=2+\ln6\approx3.792$.
  12. $y=\ln(5-x)$: need $5-x>0\Rightarrow x<5$ so domain $(-\infty,5)$; vertical asymptote $x=5$; range all real. x-int from $0=\ln(5-x)\Rightarrow x=4$. y-int at $x=0$ is $\ln5$.
