Full worked solutions (separate answer key).
  1. $11x+17=39\Rightarrow11x=22\Rightarrow x=2$.
  2. $\frac{3x-1}{5}=\frac{x+4}{2}\Rightarrow2(3x-1)=5(x+4)\Rightarrow6x-2=5x+20\Rightarrow x=22$.
  3. $x^2-13x+40=(x-5)(x-8)=0\Rightarrow x=5,8$.
  4. Let $u=x^2$. Then $u^2-10u+9=0=(u-1)(u-9)$. So $x^2=1$ or $9$, giving $x=\pm1,\pm3$.
  5. $|2x-3|=9\Rightarrow2x-3=9$ or $2x-3=-9$. So $x=6$ or $x=-3$.
  6. $-1<3x+2\le8\Rightarrow-3<3x\le6\Rightarrow-1
  7. $3x-2y+6=0\Rightarrow y=\frac32x+3$. Gradient $m=\frac32$, y-intercept $3$.
  8. Point-slope: $y-3=-4(x-2)\Rightarrow y=-4x+11$.
  9. From $x-y=1$, $y=x-1$. Substitute into $2x+y=8$: $2x+x-1=8\Rightarrow3x=9\Rightarrow x=3$, $y=2$.
  10. $f(3)=3^2-4(3)+2=-1$. $f(-1)=1+4+2=7$.
  11. For $g(x)=\frac1{x-2}$, denominator $\ne0\Rightarrow x\ne2$. So domain $(-\infty,2)\cup(2,\infty)$, range $(-\infty,0)\cup(0,\infty)$.
  12. For $x<2$, $f(0)=9$. For $x\ge2$, $f(2)=5$, $f(4)=9$.
