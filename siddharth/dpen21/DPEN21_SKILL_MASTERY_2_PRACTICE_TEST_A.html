DPEN21 Skill Mastery 2 Practice Test A

Time: 40 minutes | 12 MCQs | 2 marks each
Worked answers (separate): open answer file.

Questions

  1. [Indices] Simplify \(a^5\cdot a^{-2}\div a\).
  2. [Indices] Write with positive indices: \(\left(\frac{a^{-3}b^2}{a\,b^{-1}}\right)^2\).
  3. [Indices] Simplify \(x^{3/2}\cdot x^{1/2}\).
  4. [Linear equation] Solve \(\frac{3y+1}{4}-\frac{y-2}{3}=2\).
  5. [Quadratic formula] Solve \(x^2-6x+5=0\).
  6. [Completing square] Solve \(x^2+4x-12=0\) by completing square.
  7. [Reducible quadratic] Solve \((x^2-5x)^2-6(x^2-5x)-7=0\).
  8. [Powers equation] Solve \(2^{x+1}=32\).
  9. [Change subject] Rearrange \(\frac{1}{x}-\frac{2}{y}=1\) to make \(x\) subject.
  10. [Inequality] Solve \(-5<2x-1\le9\).
  11. [Absolute value inequality] Solve \(|x-4|<3\).
  12. [Absolute value equation] Solve \(|2x+1|=7\).
