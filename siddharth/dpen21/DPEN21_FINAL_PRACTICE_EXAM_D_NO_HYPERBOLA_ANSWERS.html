  5. Domain: \(x\neq -1\), i.e. \((-\infty,-1)\cup(-1,\infty)\). Range: \(y\neq 0\), i.e. \((-\infty,0)\cup(0,\infty)\).

  7. \(h(-2)=2(-2)=-4\Rightarrow g(-4)=\frac{1}{(-4)^2-(-4)}=\frac{1}{16+4}=\frac{1}{20}\).
  8. One real root \(\Rightarrow \Delta=a^2-4(25)=0\Rightarrow a^2=100\Rightarrow a=\pm10\).
