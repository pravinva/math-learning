Section B — Long Answer

  1. \(m=\frac{7-(-5)}{3-(-3)}=2\). Using \((3,7)\): \(7=2(3)+b\Rightarrow b=1\). So \(y=2x+1\).
  2. \(2a+4=10\Rightarrow 2a=6\Rightarrow a=3\).
  3. \(3x+y-6=0\Rightarrow y=-3x+6\), slope \(-3\); perpendicular slope \(\tfrac13\). Through \((3,2)\): \(y-2=\tfrac13(x-3)\Rightarrow x-3y+3=0\).
  4. From the second equation \(y=3x-5\). Substitute: \(x+2(3x-5)=11\Rightarrow 7x-10=11\Rightarrow x=3\); then \(y=4\).
  5. \(g(3)=3^2-1=8\Rightarrow f(8)=3(8)+2=26\). \((f\circ g)(x)=f(x^2-1)=3(x^2-1)+2=3x^2-1\).
  6. \(f(-x)=2(-x)^4-(-x)^2=2x^4-x^2=f(x)\). Even.
  7. \(y=x^2-2x-3\): vertex \(x=1,\ y=-4\Rightarrow(1,-4)\); x-intercepts \(x=-1,3\); y-intercept \((0,-3)\).
  8. Repeated root \(\Rightarrow \Delta=k^2-4(1)(9)=0\Rightarrow k^2=36\Rightarrow k=\pm 6\).
  9. \(x^2+y^2+4x-6y-3=0\Rightarrow (x+2)^2+(y-3)^2=16\). So centre \((-2,3)\), radius \(4\). Intercepts: x-axis (\(y=0\)) gives \(x=-2\pm\sqrt7\). y-axis (\(x=0\)) gives \(y=3\pm2\sqrt3\). Sketch below.
  10. \(y=-|x+2|+4\): vertex \((-2,4)\). x-intercepts from \(-|x+2|+4=0\Rightarrow |x+2|=4\Rightarrow x=-6,2\). y-intercept at \(x=0\): \(y=2\). Sketch below.
  11. \(\log_5 125=3\) (since \(5^3=125\)). Then \(5^{2t}=125=5^3\Rightarrow 2t=3\Rightarrow t=\tfrac32\).
  12. \(500\cdot 2^{t}=4000\Rightarrow 2^{t}=8=2^3\Rightarrow t=3\) hours.
  13. \(\ln[(x+3)(x-1)]=\ln 12\Rightarrow x^2+2x-3=12\Rightarrow x^2+2x-15=0\Rightarrow (x+5)(x-3)=0\). Domain requires \(x>1\), so \(x=3\) (reject \(x=-5\)).
  14. \(y=x^2-2x-3\): vertex \((1,-4)\), x-intercepts \(-1,3\), y-intercept \((0,-3)\), opens upward. Sketch below.
  15. \(y=-e^{x}+2\): y-intercept \((0,1)\); horizontal asymptote \(y=2\); decreasing. Sketch below.
  16. \(y=\ln(x+1)\): x-intercept where \(x+1=1\Rightarrow x=0\); vertical asymptote where \(x+1=0\Rightarrow x=-1\). Sketch below.
