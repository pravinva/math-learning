DPEN21 Fractional Powers Mini Test - Worked Answers

    Correct option: (B).
  3. \(y^{4/3}=81\Rightarrow (\sqrt[3]{y})^4=81\Rightarrow \sqrt[3]{y}=\pm3\Rightarrow y=(\pm3)^3=\pm27\).
    Correct option: (A).
  4. \(m^{1/3}=-2\Rightarrow m=(-2)^3=-8\).
    Correct option: (C).
  5. \(p^{5/2}=32\Rightarrow (\sqrt{p})^5=32\Rightarrow \sqrt{p}=2\Rightarrow p=4\).
    Correct option: (B).
