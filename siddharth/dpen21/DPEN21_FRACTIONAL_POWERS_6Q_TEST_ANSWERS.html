DPEN21 Fractional Powers Mini Test - Worked Answers

  1. \(3+(2a-1)^{2/5}=7\Rightarrow (2a-1)^{2/5}=4\).
    \((\sqrt[5]{2a-1})^2=4\Rightarrow \sqrt[5]{2a-1}=\pm2\).
    \(2a-1=(\pm2)^5=\pm32\Rightarrow 2a=33\) or \(2a=-31\).
    \(\therefore a=\frac{33}{2}\) or \(a=-\frac{31}{2}\).
    Correct option: (C).
  2. \(3(x-1)^{3/4}-6=18\Rightarrow 3(x-1)^{3/4}=24\Rightarrow (x-1)^{3/4}=8\).
    \((\sqrt[4]{x-1})^3=8\Rightarrow \sqrt[4]{x-1}=2\Rightarrow x-1=16\Rightarrow x=17\).
    Correct option: (B).
  3. \((y+5)^{4/3}-2=79\Rightarrow (y+5)^{4/3}=81\).
    \((\sqrt[3]{y+5})^4=81\Rightarrow \sqrt[3]{y+5}=\pm3\).
    \(y+5=(\pm3)^3=\pm27\Rightarrow y=22\) or \(y=-32\).
    Correct option: (C).
  4. \(2(m-4)^{1/3}+5=-1\Rightarrow 2(m-4)^{1/3}=-6\Rightarrow (m-4)^{1/3}=-3\).
    \(m-4=(-3)^3=-27\Rightarrow m=-23\).
    Correct option: (A).
  5. \((p+1)^{5/2}=243\Rightarrow (\sqrt{p+1})^5=243=3^5\).
    \(\sqrt{p+1}=3\Rightarrow p+1=9\Rightarrow p=8\).
    Correct option: (B).
  6. \(4(t-2)^{2/3}-3=97\Rightarrow 4(t-2)^{2/3}=100\Rightarrow (t-2)^{2/3}=25\).
    \((\sqrt[3]{t-2})^2=25\Rightarrow \sqrt[3]{t-2}=\pm5\).
    \(t-2=(\pm5)^3=\pm125\Rightarrow t=127\) or \(t=-123\).
    Correct option: (C).
