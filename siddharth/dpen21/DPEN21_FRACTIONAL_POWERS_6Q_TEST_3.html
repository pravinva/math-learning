Focus: harder shifted/scaled equations with fractional powers, including one-vs-two-solution checks.
Worked answers (separate): open answer file.

Questions

  1. Solve \(3(2a-1)^{2/5}-6=42\) for \(a\).
    (A) \(a=\frac{1025}{2}\) only
    (B) \(a=-\frac{1023}{2}\) only
    (C) \(a=\frac{1025}{2}\) or \(a=-\frac{1023}{2}\)
    (D) \(a=\pm\frac{1025}{2}\)
    (E) \(a=\pm\frac{1023}{2}\)
  2. Solve \(5(x-2)^{3/4}+9=49\) for \(x\).
    (A) \(x=14\)
    (B) \(x=18\)
    (C) \(x=22\)
    (D) \(x=-14\)
    (E) \(x=\pm18\)
  3. Solve \(2(3y+1)^{4/3}-10=152\) for \(y\).
    (A) \(y=\frac{26}{3}\) only
    (B) \(y=-\frac{28}{3}\) only
    (C) \(y=\frac{26}{3}\) or \(y=-\frac{28}{3}\)
    (D) \(y=\pm\frac{26}{3}\)
    (E) \(y=\pm\frac{28}{3}\)
  4. Solve \(6(m-4)^{1/3}+5=-7\) for \(m\).
    (A) \(m=-8\)
    (B) \(m=-4\)
    (C) \(m=0\)
    (D) \(m=4\)
    (E) \(m=\pm4\)
  5. Solve \((2p+3)^{5/2}=243\) for \(p\).
    (A) \(p=2\)
    (B) \(p=3\)
    (C) \(p=6\)
    (D) \(p=9\)
    (E) \(p=\pm3\)
  6. Solve \(4(t-1)^{2/3}+7=43\) for \(t\).
    (A) \(t=28\) only
    (B) \(t=-26\) only
    (C) \(t=28\) or \(t=-26\)
    (D) \(t=\pm27\)
    (E) \(t=\pm28\)
