Section A — Multiple Choice (12 marks)

  1. Find gradient of line \(3x-6y+9=0\).
  2. Equation of a horizontal line through \((0,-4)\).
  3. Find domain and range of \(y=\ln(x-2)\).
  4. For \(f(x)=|x+2|-3\), find \(f(-5)\).
  5. Turning point of \(y=-2x^2+8x-1\).
  6. Which quadratic has roots \(1\) and \(-6\)?
  7. Write transformed equation from \(y=|x|\): shift right 3, up 1, vertical scale 4.
  8. Circle equation with centre \((-2,5)\), radius 6.
  9. Simplify: \(2\log_a x-\log_a y+\log_a z\).
  10. Solve \(e^{2x}=15\).
  11. If \(f(x)=\log x\), write equation after shift right 4, down 2.
  12. Find y-intercept of \(y=3e^{-x}+1\).
