Section A — Multiple Choice (12 marks)

  1. Find gradient of line \(3x-6y+9=0\).
    (A) \(-\frac12\)   (B) \(\frac12\)   (C) \(2\)   (D) \(-2\)
  2. Equation of a horizontal line through \((0,-4)\).
    (A) \(x=-4\)   (B) \(y=4\)   (C) \(y=-4\)   (D) \(x=4\)
  3. Find domain and range of \(y=\ln(x-2)\).
    (A) D: \(x\gt2\), R: all real \(y\)   (B) D: \(x\ge2\), R: \(y\ge0\)   (C) D: all \(x\), R: all \(y\)   (D) D: \(x\gt0\), R: \(y\gt2\)
  4. For \(f(x)=|x+2|-3\), find \(f(-5)\).
    (A) \(-6\)   (B) \(0\)   (C) \(3\)   (D) \(6\)
  5. Turning point of \(y=-2x^2+8x-1\).
    (A) \((2,7)\)   (B) \((2,-7)\)   (C) \((-2,7)\)   (D) \((1,5)\)
  6. Which quadratic has roots \(1\) and \(-6\)?
    (A) \(x^2-5x-6\)   (B) \(x^2+5x-6\)   (C) \(x^2-7x+6\)   (D) \(x^2+7x-6\)
  7. Write transformed equation from \(y=|x|\): shift right 3, up 1, vertical scale 4.
    (A) \(4|x-3|+1\)   (B) \(4|x+3|+1\)   (C) \(|x-3|+4\)   (D) \(-4|x-3|+1\)
  8. Circle equation with centre \((-2,5)\), radius 6.
    (A) \((x+2)^2+(y-5)^2=36\)   (B) \((x-2)^2+(y+5)^2=36\)   (C) \((x+2)^2+(y-5)^2=6\)   (D) \((x-2)^2+(y+5)^2=6\)
  9. Simplify: \(2\log_a x-\log_a y+\log_a z\).
    (A) \(\log_a\!\left(\frac{x^2 z}{y}\right)\)   (B) \(\log_a\!\left(\frac{x^2 y}{z}\right)\)   (C) \(\log_a(x^2yz)\)   (D) \(\log_a\!\left(\frac{yz}{x^2}\right)\)
  10. Solve \(e^{2x}=15\).
    (A) \(x=\ln 15\)   (B) \(x=\frac{\ln 15}{2}\)   (C) \(x=2\ln 15\)   (D) \(x=\frac{15}{2}\)
  11. If \(f(x)=\log x\), write equation after shift right 4, down 2.
    (A) \(y=\log(x+4)-2\)   (B) \(y=\log(x-4)-2\)   (C) \(y=\log(x-4)+2\)   (D) \(y=\log(4-x)-2\)
  12. Find y-intercept of \(y=3e^{-x}+1\).
    (A) \(1\)   (B) \(3\)   (C) \(4\)   (D) \(e+1\)

  5. [2] Given \(f(x)=x^2-2,\ g(x)=3x+1\), find \(g(f(2))\) and \((f\circ g)(x)\).

  10. [2] Sketch \(y=-|x+2|+3\). Show vertex, x-intercepts and y-intercept.
  11. [3] In a model \(Q=80e^{0.3t}\), find \(t\) when \(Q=560\) (to 3 d.p.).
