DPEN21 CLASS SKILL MASTERY 1 TEST 2 — Worked Solutions

Full worked solutions (separate answer key).
  1. $5m-3n+mn=5(6)-3(-2)+6(-2)=30+6-12=24$.
  2. $(2x+7)(2x-3)=4x^2-6x+14x-21=4x^2+8x-21$.
  3. $(x+3)^4=x^4+4x^3(3)+6x^2(9)+4x(27)+81=x^4+12x^3+54x^2+108x+81$.
  4. $x^2-16=(x-4)(x+4)$ (difference of squares).
  5. $x^3-27=x^3-3^3=(x-3)(x^2+3x+9)$.
  6. $\frac{14a^2b}{21ab^3}=\frac{14}{21}a^{2-1}b^{1-3}=\frac{2a}{3b^2}$.
  7. $\frac{8x}{15y}\div\frac{4}{9y}=\frac{8x}{15y}\cdot\frac{9y}{4}=\frac{72xy}{60y}=\frac{6x}{5}$.
  8. $\frac{3}{x-1}-\frac{1}{x+1}=\frac{3(x+1)-(x-1)}{(x-1)(x+1)}=\frac{2x+4}{x^2-1}$.
  9. $2\sqrt{18}=6\sqrt2$, $\sqrt8=2\sqrt2$, $\sqrt{50}=5\sqrt2$. So $6\sqrt2+2\sqrt2-5\sqrt2=3\sqrt2$.
  10. $\frac{7}{3+2\sqrt2}\cdot\frac{3-2\sqrt2}{3-2\sqrt2}=\frac{7(3-2\sqrt2)}{9-8}=21-14\sqrt2$.
  11. $(a^{-2}b^3)^2\div(a^{-1}b)=a^{-4}b^6\div a^{-1}b=a^{-3}b^5=\frac{b^5}{a^3}$.
  12. $\frac{1}{x^{2/3}}=x^{-2/3}=\frac{1}{\sqrt[3]{x^2}}$.
