Squares, Cubes & Powers

A reference chart plus two drill modes: recite for fast daily reps, cold-write for honest weekly checks. Squares to 30², cubes to 12³, and powers of 2, 3, and 5.

How to use this: Recite daily, out loud, fast — the Recite Drill below is built for that (click a card to flip it, check yourself before you look). Once a week, do the Cold-Write Test with no chart in sight: type every answer from memory, then hit Check. Whatever comes back red is what tomorrow's recite session should focus on.

Powers of 5, 5¹ to 5⁴
Derive, don't memorise, these ones Powers of 4, 8, and 9 aren't worth separate memorising, they fall straight out of powers of 2 and 3: $4^n=2^{2n}$ (so $4^5=2^{10}=1024$), $8^n=2^{3n}$ (so $8^4=2^{12}=4096$), $9^n=3^{2n}$ (so $9^3=3^6=729$). If powers of 2 and 3 are solid, these come for free.
Daily Recite Drill
Click a card, say the answer out loud before it flips, then click to check. Small batches on purpose, six or so at a time, run through as many batches as you've got time for, but don't force all of them in one sitting.
Weekly Cold-Write Test
No chart, no hints. Fill in every box from memory, then hit Check. Red boxes are exactly what to drill next.
