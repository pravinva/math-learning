DPEN21 Skill Mastery Prep (Weeks 2C to 3D)

Three timed practice sets (20 questions each) focused on key exam-ready content from Week 2C, Week 2D, Week 2 Challenge, and Week 3A-3D, with Strand 1.01 aligned questions and answer keys.
Coverage: Positive/negative/fractional indices, challenge-style simplification, linear equations, equations with fractions, quadratics (factorisation / completing square / formula), reducible-to-quadratic substitution, powers, formula rearrangement, absolute-value equations, inequalities, interval notation.
