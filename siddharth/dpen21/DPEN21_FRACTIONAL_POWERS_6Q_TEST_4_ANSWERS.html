DPEN21 Fractional Powers Mini Test 4 - Worked Answers

  1. \(2(4a-3)^{2/5}+5=37\Rightarrow 2(4a-3)^{2/5}=32\Rightarrow (4a-3)^{2/5}=16\).
    \((\sqrt[5]{4a-3})^2=16\Rightarrow \sqrt[5]{4a-3}=\pm4\).
    \(4a-3=(\pm4)^5=\pm1024\Rightarrow 4a=1027\) or \(4a=-1021\).
    \(\therefore a=\frac{1027}{4}\) or \(a=-\frac{1021}{4}\).
    Correct option: (C).
  2. \(3(x+5)^{3/4}-6=18\Rightarrow 3(x+5)^{3/4}=24\Rightarrow (x+5)^{3/4}=8\).
    \((\sqrt[4]{x+5})^3=8\Rightarrow \sqrt[4]{x+5}=2\Rightarrow x+5=16\Rightarrow x=11\).
    Correct option: (A).
  3. \((5y-2)^{4/3}+12=93\Rightarrow (5y-2)^{4/3}=81\).
    \((\sqrt[3]{5y-2})^4=81\Rightarrow \sqrt[3]{5y-2}=\pm3\).
    \(5y-2=(\pm3)^3=\pm27\Rightarrow y=\frac{29}{5}\) or \(y=-5\).
    Correct option: (C).
  4. \(5(m+2)^{1/3}+4=-11\Rightarrow 5(m+2)^{1/3}=-15\Rightarrow (m+2)^{1/3}=-3\).
    Cube both sides: \(m+2=-27\Rightarrow m=-29\).
    Correct option: (A).
  5. \((3p-1)^{5/2}=32=2^5\Rightarrow (\sqrt{3p-1})^5=2^5\).
    \(\sqrt{3p-1}=2\Rightarrow 3p-1=4\Rightarrow p=\frac{5}{3}\).
    (Single real solution due to square-root branch.)
    Correct option: (B).
  6. \(6(t-4)^{2/3}+1=55\Rightarrow 6(t-4)^{2/3}=54\Rightarrow (t-4)^{2/3}=9\).
    \((\sqrt[3]{t-4})^2=9\Rightarrow \sqrt[3]{t-4}=\pm3\).
    \(t-4=(\pm3)^3=\pm27\Rightarrow t=31\) or \(t=-23\).
    Correct option: (C).
