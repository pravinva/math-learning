DPEN21 Class Final Test 4 (No Hyperbola)

Weeks 4-5-6 style mixed paper.
12 questions. Suggested time: 2 hours. Hyperbola excluded.
  1. Find line through $(4,5)$ perpendicular to $y=\frac12x-3$.
  2. Solve simultaneously: $x-2y=1$, $3x+y=14$.
  3. Find roots of $x^2-7x+10=0$ and sketch sign of the parabola.
  4. Find equation of circle from centre $(-2,1)$ radius $4$.
  5. State domain and range of $y=\sqrt{x+3}-2$.
  6. Given $f(x)=2x-1$, $g(x)=x^2+3$, find $g(f(x))$.
  7. Solve $\log_4(x+3)=2$.
  8. Solve $\log_{10}x=\log_{10}4+\log_{10}5$.
  9. Simplify: $\ln(3x)-\ln x$.
  10. Solve $9^{x}=3^{2x-1}$.
  11. Solve $e^{x-2}=6$ (3 d.p.).
  12. Sketch $y=\ln(5-x)$ with vertical asymptote, domain and range.
