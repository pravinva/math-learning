Questions

  1. Solve \(2(4a-3)^{2/5}+5=37\) for \(a\).
    (A) \(a=\frac{1027}{4}\) only
    (B) \(a=-\frac{1021}{4}\) only
    (C) \(a=\frac{1027}{4}\) or \(a=-\frac{1021}{4}\)
    (D) \(a=\pm\frac{1027}{4}\)
    (E) \(a=\pm\frac{1021}{4}\)
  2. Solve \(3(x+5)^{3/4}-6=18\) for \(x\).
    (A) \(x=11\)
    (B) \(x=16\)
    (C) \(x=21\)
    (D) \(x=-11\)
    (E) \(x=\pm16\)
  3. Solve \((5y-2)^{4/3}+12=93\) for \(y\).
    (A) \(y=\frac{29}{5}\) only
    (B) \(y=-5\) only
    (C) \(y=\frac{29}{5}\) or \(y=-5\)
    (D) \(y=\pm\frac{29}{5}\)
    (E) \(y=\pm5\)
  4. Solve \(5(m+2)^{1/3}+4=-11\) for \(m\).
    (A) \(m=-29\)
    (B) \(m=-27\)
    (C) \(m=-25\)
    (D) \(m=25\)
    (E) \(m=\pm25\)
  5. Solve \((3p-1)^{5/2}=32\) for \(p\).
    (A) \(p=1\)
    (B) \(p=\frac{5}{3}\)
    (C) \(p=\frac{11}{3}\)
    (D) \(p=5\)
    (E) \(p=\pm\frac{11}{3}\)
  6. Solve \(6(t-4)^{2/3}+1=55\) for \(t\).
    (A) \(t=31\) only
    (B) \(t=-23\) only
    (C) \(t=31\) or \(t=-23\)
    (D) \(t=\pm27\)
    (E) \(t=\pm31\)
