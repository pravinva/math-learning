DPEN21 CLASS FINAL TEST 2 NO HYPERBOLA — Worked Solutions

Full worked solutions (separate answer key).
  1. $3x-y+2=0\Rightarrow y=3x+2$ has slope 3. Through $(-1,4)$: $y-4=3(x+1)\Rightarrow y=3x+7$.
  2. From $4x+y=11$, $y=11-4x$. Substitute into $2x-3y=-1$: $2x-33+12x=-1\Rightarrow14x=32\Rightarrow x=\frac{16}{7}$, $y=\frac{13}{7}$.
  3. $y=x^2-6x+10=(x-3)^2+1$. Minimum value is $1$ at $x=3$.
  4. Circle: $(x-3)^2+(y+2)^2=25$.
  5. $y=|x+2|-3$: domain all real. Minimum at $x=-2$ gives $y=-3$. Range $[-3,\infty)$.
  6. $f(g(x))=\frac1{x+4}$.
  7. $\log_2x=5\Rightarrow x=2^5=32$.
  8. $\log(x+1)+\log(x-1)=1\Rightarrow\log[(x+1)(x-1)]=1$. So $x^2-1=10$, $x^2=11$, and domain needs $x>1$, so $x=\sqrt{11}$.
  9. $\ln(e^{3x})-2\ln(e^x)=3x-2x=x$.
  10. $25^{-1}=5^{-2}$. So $5^{x-2}=5^{-2}\Rightarrow x-2=-2\Rightarrow x=0$.
  11. $2e^x-7=0\Rightarrow e^x=\frac72\Rightarrow x=\ln\frac72\approx1.253$.
  12. $y=\ln(x-2)+1$: vertical asymptote $x=2$, domain $(2,\infty)$, range $(-\infty,\infty)$. x-int from $0=\ln(x-2)+1\Rightarrow x=2+e^{-1}$. No y-int (outside domain).
