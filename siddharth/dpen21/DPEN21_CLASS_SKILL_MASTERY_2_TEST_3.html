DPEN21 Class Skill Mastery Test 2C

Built from Week class-question styles.
12 questions. Suggested time: 50 minutes.
  1. Solve: $7-3m=2m+12$.
  2. Solve: $\dfrac{x+1}{4}+\dfrac{x-3}{6}=1$.
  3. Solve: $3x^2+2x-8=0$.
  4. Solve reducible equation: $(x^2+1)^2-5(x^2+1)+6=0$.
  5. Solve: $|3x+1|=8$.
  6. Solve in interval notation: $|2x-5|\le 7$.
  7. Find equation of line with x-intercept $4$ and y-intercept $-2$.
  8. Is line $2x-3y=6$ perpendicular to $3x+2y=1$?
  9. Find intersection point of $y=3x-4$ and $y=-x+8$.
  10. Given $F(x)=\dfrac{x+1}{x-2}$, find $F(4)$.
  11. For $f(x)=x^2+1$ and $g(x)=2x-3$, find $f(g(x))$.
  12. State domain and range of $y=|x-4|+1$.
