DPEN21 Class Skill Mastery Test 2D

Built from Week class-question styles.
12 questions. Suggested time: 50 minutes.
  1. Solve: $2(5x-1)-3(x+4)=7$.
  2. Solve: $\dfrac{2x-1}{3}=\dfrac{x+5}{4}$.
  3. Solve: $x^2+12x+20=0$.
  4. Solve by substitution $u=2x-1$: (2x-1)^2-6(2x-1)+5=0.
  5. Solve: $|x-7|+2=6$.
  6. Solve in interval notation: $\dfrac{x-1}{2}<3$.
  7. Find gradient of line through $(0,-2)$ and $(7,1)$.
  8. Find line perpendicular to $y=-\frac13x+5$ through $(6,2)$.
  9. Solve simultaneously: $5x-2y=9$, $3x+y=7$.
  10. Given $f(x)=x^2-2x$ and $g(x)=x+3$, find $g(f(2))$.
  11. State whether $f(x)=x^4+2$ is odd, even, or neither.
  12. Find domain of $y=\dfrac{1}{x^2-9}$.
