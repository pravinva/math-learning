Section B — Long Answer

  1. \(m=\frac{5-(-3)}{6-2}=2\Rightarrow y=2x-7\) (using \((2,-3)\): \(-3=4+b\Rightarrow b=-7\)).
  2. \(a+5(2)=12\Rightarrow a=2\).
  3. \(y-5x=3\Rightarrow y=5x+3\), slope \(5\); perpendicular slope \(-\frac15\). Through \((0,3)\): \(y-3=-\frac15(x-0)\Rightarrow x+5y-15=0\).
  4. Add: \(6x=18\Rightarrow x=3\); then \(4(3)+y=14\Rightarrow y=2\).
  5. Domain: \(x\neq -2\), i.e. \((-\infty,-2)\cup(-2,\infty)\). Range: \(y\neq 0\), i.e. \((-\infty,0)\cup(0,\infty)\).
  6. \(f(-x)=(-x)^4+1=x^4+1=f(x)\). Even.
  7. \(h(-2)=3(-2)=-6\Rightarrow g(-6)=\frac{1}{(-6)^2-2(-6)}=\frac{1}{36+12}=\frac{1}{48}\).
  8. One real root \(\Rightarrow \Delta=a^2-4(49)=0\Rightarrow a^2=196\Rightarrow a=\pm14\).
  9. \(y=-x^2+4\): vertex \((0,4)\); x-intercepts \(x=\pm2\); y-intercept \((0,4)\). Sketch below.
  10. \(y=|x+2|+1\): vertex \((-2,1)\), y-intercept \((0,3)\), opens upward. Sketch below.
  11. \(x^2+y^2+2x=8\Rightarrow (x+1)^2+y^2=9\). Centre \((-1,0)\), radius \(3\).
  12. x-intercepts (\(y=0\)): \((x+1)^2=9\Rightarrow x=2\) or \(x=-4\). y-intercepts (\(x=0\)): \(1+y^2=9\Rightarrow y=\pm2\sqrt2\). Sketch below.
  13. \(\log_{2}128=7\) (since \(2^7=128\)).
  14. \(5e^{x-4}=10\Rightarrow e^{x-4}=2\Rightarrow x-4=\ln2\Rightarrow x=4+\ln2\approx4.693\).
  15. \(y=\ln(x-1)\): x-intercept where \(\ln(x-1)=0\Rightarrow x=2\); vertical asymptote \(x=1\). Sketch below.
  16. \(y=e^{x}+1\): y-intercept \((0,2)\); horizontal asymptote \(y=1\). Sketch below.
