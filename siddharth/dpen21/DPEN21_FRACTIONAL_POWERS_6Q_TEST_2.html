DPEN21 Fractional Powers Mini Test 2

Time: 20 minutes | 6 MCQs | 2 marks each
Focus: solving equations with fractional powers (shifted/scaled forms).
Worked answers (separate): open answer file.

Questions

  1. Solve \(5+(3a+2)^{2/5}=14\) for \(a\).
    (A) \(a=\frac{241}{3}\) only
    (B) \(a=-\frac{245}{3}\) only
    (C) \(a=\frac{241}{3}\) or \(a=-\frac{245}{3}\)
    (D) \(a=\pm\frac{241}{3}\)
    (E) \(a=\pm\frac{245}{3}\)
  2. Solve \(2(x+4)^{3/4}+1=17\) for \(x\).
    (A) \(x=8\)
    (B) \(x=12\)
    (C) \(x=16\)
    (D) \(x=-12\)
    (E) \(x=\pm12\)
  3. Solve \((2y-1)^{4/3}-7=74\) for \(y\).
    (A) \(y=14\) only
    (B) \(y=-13\) only
    (C) \(y=14\) or \(y=-13\)
    (D) \(y=\pm13\)
    (E) \(y=\pm14\)
  4. Solve \(4(m+1)^{1/3}-2=10\) for \(m\).
    (A) \(m=8\)
    (B) \(m=26\)
    (C) \(m=27\)
    (D) \(m=-26\)
    (E) \(m=\pm26\)
  5. Solve \((p-5)^{5/2}=1024\) for \(p\).
    (A) \(p=9\)
    (B) \(p=13\)
    (C) \(p=21\)
    (D) \(p=37\)
    (E) \(p=\pm21\)
  6. Solve \(3(t+6)^{2/3}+4=79\) for \(t\).
    (A) \(t=119\) only
    (B) \(t=-131\) only
    (C) \(t=119\) or \(t=-131\)
    (D) \(t=\pm125\)
    (E) \(t=\pm131\)
