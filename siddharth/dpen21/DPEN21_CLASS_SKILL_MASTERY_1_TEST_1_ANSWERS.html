DPEN21 CLASS SKILL MASTERY 1 TEST 1 — Worked Solutions

Full worked solutions (separate answer key).
  1. Given $a=4,b=-3,c=\frac14$: $a-b=7$, so $\frac{a-b}{c}=\frac{7}{1/4}=28$. Also $ab=4(-3)=-12$. Therefore $28+(-12)=16$.
  2. $(3x-1)(x+5)=3x^2+15x-x-5=3x^2+14x-5$.
  3. Using coefficients $1,5,10,10,5,1$: $(2x-1)^5=(2x)^5-5(2x)^4+10(2x)^3-10(2x)^2+5(2x)-1=32x^5-80x^4+80x^3-40x^2+10x-1$.
  4. $6x^2-15x=3x(2x-5)$ by taking out HCF $3x$.
  5. $3ax+6ay+bx+2by=(3ax+6ay)+(bx+2by)=3a(x+2y)+b(x+2y)=(a+b)(3x+2y)$.
  6. $\frac{12x^3y^2}{18x^2y}=\frac{12}{18}x^{3-2}y^{2-1}=\frac23xy$.
  7. $\left(\frac{3x^2}{5y}\right)\left(\frac{10y^2}{9x}\right)=\frac{30x^2y^2}{45xy}=\frac23xy$.
  8. Common denominator $2x$: $\frac{2}{x}=\frac{4}{2x}$, so $\frac{4}{2x}+\frac{3}{2x}=\frac{7}{2x}$.
  9. $\sqrt{75}=5\sqrt3$, $\sqrt{12}=2\sqrt3$, $\sqrt{27}=3\sqrt3$. Hence $5\sqrt3-2\sqrt3+3\sqrt3=6\sqrt3$.
  10. Multiply by conjugate: $\frac{5}{2-\sqrt3}\cdot\frac{2+\sqrt3}{2+\sqrt3}=\frac{5(2+\sqrt3)}{4-3}=10+5\sqrt3$.
  11. $\frac{x^{-3}y^2}{x^{-1}y^{-4}}=x^{-3-(-1)}y^{2-(-4)}=x^{-2}y^6=\frac{y^6}{x^2}$.
  12. $x^{3/2}y^{1/2}=x\sqrt{x}\sqrt{y}=x\sqrt{xy}$.
