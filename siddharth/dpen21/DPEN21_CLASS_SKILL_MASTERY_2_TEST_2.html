DPEN21 Class Skill Mastery Test 2B

Built from Week class-question styles.
12 questions. Suggested time: 50 minutes.
  1. Solve: $4(3x-2)=5x+7$.
  2. Solve: $\dfrac{5}{3}x-\dfrac{2}{3}=4$.
  3. Solve: $2x^2-9x-18=0$.
  4. Solve by substitution $u=x-1$: $(x-1)^2-7(x-1)+12=0$.
  5. Solve: $|x+5|=3x-1$.
  6. Solve in interval notation: $5-2x\ge 9$.
  7. Find slope between $(-3,1)$ and $(1,9)$.
  8. Find equation of line through $(-1,5)$ parallel to $y=2x-3$.
  9. Solve simultaneously: $3x+4y=7$, $x-2y=5$.
  10. Given $h(t)=2t^2-3t$, find $h(2a)$.
  11. State whether $f(x)=x^3-x$ is odd, even, or neither.
  12. Find domain of $y=\sqrt{2x-5}$.
