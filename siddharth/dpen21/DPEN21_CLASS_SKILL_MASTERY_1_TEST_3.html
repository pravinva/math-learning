DPEN21 Class Skill Mastery Test 1C

Built from Week class-question styles.
12 questions. Suggested time: 50 minutes.
  1. Evaluate when $x=-2$: $3x^2-4x+1$.
  2. Expand and simplify $(x-4)^2-(x+1)(x-1)$.
  3. Use Pascal's triangle to expand $(3x+2)^4$.
  4. Factorise completely: $12x^2-27x+15$.
  5. Factorise by grouping: $x^2+3x+2x+6$.
  6. Simplify: $\dfrac{20x^2y^3}{25xy^2}$.
  7. Simplify: $\left(\dfrac{5a}{6b}\right)\times\left(\dfrac{9b^2}{10a^2}\right)$.
  8. Simplify: $\dfrac{x}{x+2}+\dfrac{2}{x+2}$.
  9. Simplify: $\sqrt{48}-2\sqrt3+\sqrt{27}$.
  10. Rationalise the denominator: $\dfrac{4}{\sqrt5-1}$.
  11. Write with positive indices: $\dfrac{p^{-4}q^{-1}}{p^{-2}q^{-5}}$.
  12. Write without fractional indices: $(16x)^{1/4}$.
