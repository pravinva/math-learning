Section B — Long Answer

  1. \(m=\frac{5-(-3)}{4-0}=2\Rightarrow y=2x-3\).
  2. \(a+2(3)=10\Rightarrow a=4\).
  3. \(y-3x=1\Rightarrow y=3x+1\), slope \(3\); perpendicular slope \(-\frac13\). Through \((1,1)\): \(y-1=-\frac13(x-1)\Rightarrow x+3y-4=0\).
  4. Add: \(6x=18\Rightarrow x=3\); then \(6+3y=12\Rightarrow y=2\).
  5. Domain: \(x\neq 2\), i.e. \((-\infty,2)\cup(2,\infty)\). Range: \(y\neq 0\), i.e. \((-\infty,0)\cup(0,\infty)\).
  6. \(f(-x)=(-x)^4-2(-x)^2=x^4-2x^2=f(x)\). Even.
  7. \(h(-2)=3(-2)=-6\Rightarrow g(-6)=\frac{1}{(-6)^2+2(-6)}=\frac{1}{36-12}=\frac{1}{24}\).
  8. One real root \(\Rightarrow \Delta=a^2-4(36)=0\Rightarrow a^2=144\Rightarrow a=\pm12\).

  10. \(y=|x+1|+2\): vertex \((-1,2)\), y-intercept \((0,3)\), opens upward. Sketch below.
  11. \(x^2+y^2-2y=3\Rightarrow x^2+(y-1)^2=4\). Centre \((0,1)\), radius \(2\).
  12. x-intercepts (\(y=0\)): \(x^2+1=4\Rightarrow x=\pm\sqrt3\). y-intercepts (\(x=0\)): \((y-1)^2=4\Rightarrow y=3\) or \(y=-1\). Sketch below.
  13. \(\log_{\sqrt2}32=\frac{\ln32}{\ln\sqrt2}=\frac{5\ln2}{\tfrac12\ln2}=10\).
  14. \(3e^{x-5}=9\Rightarrow e^{x-5}=3\Rightarrow x-5=\ln3\Rightarrow x=5+\ln3\approx6.099\).
