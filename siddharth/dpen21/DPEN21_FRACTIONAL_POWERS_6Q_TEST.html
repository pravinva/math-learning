DPEN21 Fractional Powers Mini Test

Time: 20 minutes | 6 MCQs | 2 marks each
Focus: solving equations with fractional powers.
Worked answers (separate): open answer file.

Questions

  1. Solve \(3+(2a-1)^{2/5}=7\) for \(a\).
    (A) \(a=\frac{33}{2}\) only
    (B) \(a=-\frac{31}{2}\) only
    (C) \(a=\frac{33}{2}\) or \(a=-\frac{31}{2}\)
    (D) \(a=\pm\frac{31}{2}\)
    (E) \(a=\pm\frac{33}{2}\)
  2. Solve \(3(x-1)^{3/4}-6=18\) for \(x\).
    (A) \(x=16\)
    (B) \(x=17\)
    (C) \(x=-15\)
    (D) \(x=\pm17\)
    (E) \(x=65\)
  3. Solve \((y+5)^{4/3}-2=79\) for \(y\).
    (A) \(y=22\) only
    (B) \(y=-32\) only
    (C) \(y=22\) or \(y=-32\)
    (D) \(y=\pm22\)
    (E) \(y=\pm32\)
  4. Solve \(2(m-4)^{1/3}+5=-1\) for \(m\).
    (A) \(m=-23\)
    (B) \(m=23\)
    (C) \(m=-27\)
    (D) \(m=31\)
    (E) \(m=\pm23\)
  5. Solve \((p+1)^{5/2}=243\) for \(p\).
    (A) \(p=4\)
    (B) \(p=8\)
    (C) \(p=9\)
    (D) \(p=\pm8\)
    (E) \(p=26\)
  6. Solve \(4(t-2)^{2/3}-3=97\) for \(t\).
    (A) \(t=127\) only
    (B) \(t=-123\) only
    (C) \(t=127\) or \(t=-123\)
    (D) \(t=\pm125\)
    (E) \(t=\pm127\)
