DPEN21 Fractional Powers Mini Test 3 - Worked Answers

  1. \(3(2a-1)^{2/5}-6=42\Rightarrow 3(2a-1)^{2/5}=48\Rightarrow (2a-1)^{2/5}=16\).
    \((\sqrt[5]{2a-1})^2=16\Rightarrow \sqrt[5]{2a-1}=\pm4\) (even numerator gives two cases).
    \(2a-1=(\pm4)^5=\pm1024\Rightarrow 2a=1025\) or \(2a=-1023\).
    \(\therefore a=\frac{1025}{2}\) or \(a=-\frac{1023}{2}\).
    Correct option: (C).
  2. \(5(x-2)^{3/4}+9=49\Rightarrow 5(x-2)^{3/4}=40\Rightarrow (x-2)^{3/4}=8\).
    \((\sqrt[4]{x-2})^3=8\Rightarrow \sqrt[4]{x-2}=2\Rightarrow x-2=16\Rightarrow x=18\).
    Correct option: (B).
  3. \(2(3y+1)^{4/3}-10=152\Rightarrow 2(3y+1)^{4/3}=162\Rightarrow (3y+1)^{4/3}=81\).
    \((\sqrt[3]{3y+1})^4=81\Rightarrow \sqrt[3]{3y+1}=\pm3\) (even numerator gives two real branches).
    \(3y+1=(\pm3)^3=\pm27\Rightarrow y=\frac{26}{3}\) or \(y=-\frac{28}{3}\).
    Correct option: (C).
  4. \(6(m-4)^{1/3}+5=-7\Rightarrow 6(m-4)^{1/3}=-12\Rightarrow (m-4)^{1/3}=-2\).
    Cube both sides: \(m-4=-8\Rightarrow m=-4\).
    Correct option: (B).
  5. \((2p+3)^{5/2}=243=3^5\Rightarrow (\sqrt{2p+3})^5=3^5\).
    \(\sqrt{2p+3}=3\Rightarrow 2p+3=9\Rightarrow p=3\).
    (Single real solution because \(\sqrt{2p+3}\ge0\).)
    Correct option: (B).
  6. \(4(t-1)^{2/3}+7=43\Rightarrow 4(t-1)^{2/3}=36\Rightarrow (t-1)^{2/3}=9\).
    \((\sqrt[3]{t-1})^2=9\Rightarrow \sqrt[3]{t-1}=\pm3\).
    \(t-1=(\pm3)^3=\pm27\Rightarrow t=28\) or \(t=-26\).
    Correct option: (C).
