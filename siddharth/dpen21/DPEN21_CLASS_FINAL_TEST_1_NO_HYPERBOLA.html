DPEN21 Class Final Test 1 (No Hyperbola)

Weeks 4-5-6 style mixed paper.
12 questions. Suggested time: 2 hours. Hyperbola excluded.
  1. Find equation of line through $(2,-1)$ and $(6,7)$.
  2. Find intersection of $2x-3y=7$ and $x+y=5$.
  3. Sketch $y=(x-1)^2-4$ and state vertex and intercepts.
  4. Find centre and radius of $x^2+y^2-6x+8y-11=0$.
  5. State domain and range of $y=\sqrt{9-x^2}$.
  6. Given $f(x)=x^2+1$, $g(x)=3x-2$, find $f(g(x))$ and $g(f(1))$.
  7. Solve $\log_5(x-1)=2$.
  8. Solve $\ln(2x+1)=3$.
  9. Simplify: $\log_{10}40-\log_{10}5+2\log_{10}x$.
  10. Solve $3^{2x-1}=27$.
  11. Solve $e^{x+1}=20$ (3 d.p.).
  12. Sketch $y=e^{x-2}-1$ showing asymptote, intercepts, domain and range.
