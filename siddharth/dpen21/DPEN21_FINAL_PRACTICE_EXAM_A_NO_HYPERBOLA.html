DPEN21 Final Practice Exam A (No Hyperbola)

Time allowed: 2 hours | Total: 48 marks | Calculator allowed
No hyperbola questions included. Worked solutions are in a separate file: Exam A Worked Answers.

Section A — Multiple Choice (12 marks)

  1. Find the gradient of the line through \((2,-1)\) and \((8,11)\).
  2. The graph \(x=-3\) is: (A) horizontal line (B) vertical line (C) parabola (D) circle.
  3. Find the domain and range of \(y=\sqrt{x+1}-2\).
  4. For \(f(x)=\begin{cases}x+2,&x<0\\x^2-1,&x\ge0\end{cases}\), evaluate \(f(-3)+2f(2)\).
  5. Find the turning point of \(y=3x^2-12x+7\).
  6. Which equation has x-intercepts at \(-2\) and \(5\)?
  7. Write equation of absolute-value graph with vertex \((1,-4)\), opening upward with scale 2.
  8. Circle with centre \((2,-3)\), radius \(4\): write standard form equation.
  9. Simplify to one logarithm: \(\log_a x + 2\log_a y - \log_a z\).
  10. Solve \(\log_{10}(x-1)=2\).
  11. If \(g(x)=e^x\), write equation after shifting 2 left and 3 down.
  12. Find x-intercept of \(y=\ln(x+4)-1\).

Section B — Long Answer (36 marks)

  1. [3] Find equation of the line through \((-1,4)\) and \((3,-2)\) in gradient-intercept form.
  2. [3] Find equation of line through \((2,5)\) perpendicular to \(4x-2y+7=0\).
  3. [3] Solve simultaneously: \[ \begin{cases} 3x+2y=17\\ 5x-2y=7 \end{cases} \]
  4. [3] State whether \(f(x)=x^4-2x^2\) is odd, even, or neither (show check).
  5. [3] Given \(f(x)=2x-1,\ g(x)=x^2+3\), find \(f(g(-2))\) and \((g\circ f)(x)\).
  6. [4] For \(y=-x^2+6x-5\), find vertex, x-intercepts, y-intercept, and sketch features.
  7. [3] Solve \(x^2-7x+12=0\) and state nature of roots using discriminant.
  8. [3] Find centre and radius of \(x^2+y^2-6x+8y+9=0\).
  9. [3] Find intercepts of the circle in Q20.
  10. [4] Solve \(3^{x+1}=20\) for \(x\) (3 d.p.).
  11. [4] Solve \(\ln(x-2)+\ln(x+1)=\ln 12\), including domain restriction.
  12. [3] Sketch \(y=e^{-x}+2\): y-intercept, horizontal asymptote, and one extra point.
