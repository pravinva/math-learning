Questions

  7. [Power-4 substitution] Solve \(x^4-5x^2-36=0\) using \(u=x^2\).
  8. [Powers equation] Solve \(4^{x+1}=64\).
  9. [Change subject] Rearrange \(\frac{5}{x}+\frac{2}{y}=4\) for \(x\).
  10. [Inequality] Solve \(2<5x-3\le17\).
  11. [Absolute value inequality] Solve \(|2x+3|<7\).
