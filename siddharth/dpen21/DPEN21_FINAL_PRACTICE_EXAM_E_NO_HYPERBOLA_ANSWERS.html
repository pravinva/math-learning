Section B — Long Answer

  1. Midpoint \(=\left(\frac{2+8}{2},\frac{3+11}{2}\right)=(5,7)\). Length \(=\sqrt{(8-2)^2+(11-3)^2}=\sqrt{36+64}=\sqrt{100}=10\).
  2. The y-axis is \(x=0\): \(y=2(0)-3=-3\). So \(P=(0,-3)\).
  3. \(2x-4y+3=0\Rightarrow y=\tfrac12 x+\tfrac34\), slope \(\tfrac12\); perpendicular slope \(-2\). Through \((1,4)\): \(y-4=-2(x-1)\Rightarrow 2x+y-6=0\).
  4. Add the equations: \(4x=16\Rightarrow x=4\); then \(4+2y=12\Rightarrow y=4\).
  5. \(f(2)=2^2+1=5\Rightarrow g(5)=2(5)-3=7\). \((f\circ g)(x)=f(2x-3)=(2x-3)^2+1=4x^2-12x+10\).
  6. \(f(-x)=(-x)^3+2(-x)=-x^3-2x=-(x^3+2x)=-f(x)\). Odd.
  7. \(y=2x^2-8x+6\): vertex \(x=2,\ y=-2\Rightarrow(2,-2)\); x-intercepts \(x=1,3\); y-intercept \((0,6)\).
  8. Repeated root \(\Rightarrow \Delta=(-6)^2-4(1)c=0\Rightarrow 36-4c=0\Rightarrow c=9\).
  9. \(x^2+y^2-2x-4y-4=0\Rightarrow (x-1)^2+(y-2)^2=4+1+4=9\). Centre \((1,2)\), radius \(3\).
  10. x-intercepts (\(y=0\)): \((x-1)^2+4=9\Rightarrow (x-1)^2=5\Rightarrow x=1\pm\sqrt5\). y-intercepts (\(x=0\)): \(1+(y-2)^2=9\Rightarrow y=2\pm2\sqrt2\).
  11. \(\log_2 64=6\) (since \(2^6=64\)). Then \(2^{3t}=64=2^6\Rightarrow 3t=6\Rightarrow t=2\).
  12. \(100e^{0.5t}=300\Rightarrow e^{0.5t}=3\Rightarrow 0.5t=\ln 3\Rightarrow t=2\ln 3\approx2.197\) years.
  13. \(\ln(2x-1)=\ln(x+4)\Rightarrow 2x-1=x+4\Rightarrow x=5\). Domain requires \(2x-1>0\) and \(x+4>0\); \(x=5\) is valid.
