Section B — Long Answer

  1. \(m=\frac{7-(-1)}{2-(-2)}=2\Rightarrow y=2x+3\) (using \((2,7)\): \(7=4+b\Rightarrow b=3\)).
  2. \(a+3=8\Rightarrow a=5\).
  3. \(y-x=2\Rightarrow y=x+2\), slope \(1\); perpendicular slope \(-1\). Through \((1,1)\): \(y-1=-1(x-1)\Rightarrow x+y-2=0\).
  4. Add: \(4x=12\Rightarrow x=3\); then \(3-y=2\Rightarrow y=1\).
  5. Domain: \(x\neq 3\), i.e. \((-\infty,3)\cup(3,\infty)\). Range: \(y\neq 0\), i.e. \((-\infty,0)\cup(0,\infty)\).
  6. \(f(-x)=(-x)^3+(-x)=-x^3-x=-(x^3+x)=-f(x)\). Odd.
  7. \(h(-2)=2(-2)=-4\Rightarrow g(-4)=\frac{1}{(-4)^2+(-4)}=\frac{1}{16-4}=\frac{1}{12}\).
  8. One real root \(\Rightarrow \Delta=a^2-4(16)=0\Rightarrow a^2=64\Rightarrow a=\pm8\).

  10. \(y=|x-3|+1\): vertex \((3,1)\), y-intercept \((0,4)\), opens upward. Sketch below.
  11. \(x^2+y^2-6x=7\Rightarrow (x-3)^2+y^2=16\). Centre \((3,0)\), radius \(4\).
  12. x-intercepts (\(y=0\)): \((x-3)^2=16\Rightarrow x=7\) or \(x=-1\). y-intercepts (\(x=0\)): \(9+y^2=16\Rightarrow y=\pm\sqrt7\). Sketch below.
  13. \(\log_{3}81=4\) (since \(3^4=81\)).
  14. \(4e^{x-2}=12\Rightarrow e^{x-2}=3\Rightarrow x-2=\ln3\Rightarrow x=2+\ln3\approx3.099\).
