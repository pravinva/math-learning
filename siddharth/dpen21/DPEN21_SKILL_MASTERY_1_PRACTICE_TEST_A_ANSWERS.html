Skill Mastery 1 Practice Test A — Worked Answers

  1. \(3x^2-2x+8+4x^2-4=7x^2-2x+4\).
  2. \(5a^2-2(a^2-6ab+9b^2)=3a^2+12ab-18b^2\).
  3. \((1+1)^6=64\).
  4. \((3y-x)^4=81y^4-108y^3x+54y^2x^2-12yx^3+x^4\).
  5. \(6a^2-15-12a+30=6a^2-12a+15=3(2a^2-4a+5)\) (cannot factor further over integers).
  6. \(a(4c-3d)+2b(4c-3d)=(a+2b)(4c-3d)\).
  7. \([(4x+3y)-(x-2y)][(4x+3y)+(x-2y)]=(3x+5y)(5x+y)\).
  8. \(3x^2-11x-4=(3x+1)(x-4)\).
  9. \(\frac{2x(x^2-4)}{x^2-4}=2x,\ x\ne\pm2\).
  10. \(\frac{3(x-2)-2(x+1)}{(x+1)(x-2)}=\frac{x-8}{(x+1)(x-2)}\).
  11. \(5(2\sqrt3)-2(3\sqrt3)+5\sqrt3=9\sqrt3\).
  12. \(\frac{1}{4+\sqrt3}\cdot\frac{4-\sqrt3}{4-\sqrt3}=\frac{4-\sqrt3}{13}\).
