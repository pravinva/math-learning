DPEN21 Skill Mastery 1 Practice Test A

Time: 40 minutes | 12 MCQs | 2 marks each
Worked answers (separate): open answer file.

Questions

  1. [Expansion] Simplify \(3x^2-2(x-4)+4(x^2-1)\).
  2. [Expansion] Expand and simplify \(5a^2-2(a-3b)^2\).
  3. [Pascal] Sum of coefficients in \((a+b)^6\).
  4. [Pascal] Expand \((3y-x)^4\).
  5. [Factorising] Expand then factorise \(3(2a^2-5)-12a+30\).
  6. [Factorising by grouping] Factorise \(4ac-3ad+8bc-6bd\).
  7. [Difference of squares] Factorise \((4x+3y)^2-(x-2y)^2\).
  8. [Quadratic] Factorise completely \(3x^2-11x-4\).
  9. [Algebraic fractions] Simplify \(\frac{2x^3-8x}{x^2-4}\).
  10. [Fractions ops] Simplify \(\frac{3}{x+1}-\frac{2}{x-2}\).
  11. [Surds] Simplify \(5\sqrt{12}-2\sqrt{27}+\sqrt{75}\).
  12. [Rationalise] Rationalise \(\frac{1}{4+\sqrt3}\).
