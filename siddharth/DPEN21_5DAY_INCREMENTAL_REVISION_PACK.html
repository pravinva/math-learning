DPEN21 5-Day Incremental Revision Pack

Circles, absolute value, discriminant, parabola, hyperbola, lines, logs, exponentials, and function composition
How to use: Do each day in order. Keep old topics alive while adding new ones. Complete questions first; check answers at the end.

Day 1

Focus: Circles (intro) + memory anchors for logs and exponentials
Q1 (Log):

Solve \( \log_2 x + \log_2 4 = 5 \).

Q2 (Exponential graph):

Sketch \(y=2^x\), and label y-intercept, horizontal asymptote, domain, and range.

Q3 (Circle):

For \((x-2)^2+(y+1)^2=9\), find centre and radius.

Day 2

Focus: Circles (intercepts + domain/range), plus daily log/exp revision
Q1 (Log):

Solve \( \log x - \log 2 = 1 \).

Q2 (Exponential graph):

Sketch \(y=2^{x-1}-3\), label asymptote and intercepts.

Q3 (Circle):

Find domain and range of \((x-1)^2+(y+2)^2=16\).

Day 3

Focus: Composite/odd-even functions + 2 absolute value questions, while retaining log/exp/circle
Q1 (Log):

Solve \( \log_3(2x-1)=2 \).

Q2 (Exponential):

Solve \( e^x=25 \) and leave exact form.

Q3 (Circle):

Find x-intercepts of \((x-1)^2+y^2=4\).

Q4 (Functions):

If \(f(x)=x^3-x\), \(g(x)=x^2+1\): state odd/even for each and find \((f\circ g)(x)\).

Q5 (Absolute value 1):

Solve \( |x-3|=5 \).

Q6 (Absolute value 2):

Find vertex, x-intercepts, and range of \(y=|x-2|-3\).

Day 4

Focus: Add parabola + discriminant, keep all previous topics alive
Q1 (Log):

Solve \( \log x-\log(x-1)=1 \) with domain check.

Q2 (Exponential):

Solve \(2^{x+1}=4^{3x-2}\).

Q3 (Circle):

Convert \(x^2+y^2-4x+6y+9=0\) to standard form and identify centre/radius.

Q4 (Functions):

Determine whether \(h(x)=x^4+1\) is odd, even, or neither.

Q5 (Absolute value):

Solve \( |2x-3| \ge 5 \).

Q6 (Parabola graph):

Sketch \(y=(x-1)^2-4\). Label vertex, axis of symmetry, and intercepts.

Q7 (Discriminant):

For \(x^2-6x+13=0\), use discriminant to classify roots.

Day 5

Focus: Add hyperbola + lines, full mixed revision
Q1 (Log):

Solve \( \log_2 16+\log_2 x=\log_2 32 \).

Q2 (Exponential graph):

Sketch \(y=3^x\), state domain/range/asymptote.

Q3 (Circle):

For \((x+2)^2+(y-1)^2=9\), find domain and range.

Q4 (Functions):

If \(f(x)=|x|\), \(g(x)=x-2\), find \((f\circ g)(x)\) and where it equals 0.

Q5 (Parabola):

Find nature of roots of \(x^2-4x+4=0\), then state vertex of \(y=x^2-4x+4\).

Q6 (Hyperbola):

For \(y=2+\frac{4}{x-3}\), find centre, asymptotes, intercepts, domain, and range.

Q7 (Lines):

Find equation, gradient, and intercepts of line through \((1,2)\), \((-1,3)\).

Q8 (Lines):

Find line through \((2,3)\) parallel to \(3x-7y=0\).

Answer Key

Day 1

  • Q1: \(x=8\)
  • Q2: y-intercept \((0,1)\), asymptote \(y=0\), domain \(\mathbb{R}\), range \((0,\infty)\)
  • Q3: Centre \((2,-1)\), radius \(3\)

Day 2

  • Q1: \(x=20\)
  • Q2: asymptote \(y=-3\), y-int \((0,-\frac52)\), x-int \((1+\log_2 3,0)\)
  • Q3: Domain \([-3,5]\), range \([-6,2]\)

Day 3

  • Q1: \(x=5\)
  • Q2: \(x=\ln 25\)
  • Q3: \((-1,0)\), \((3,0)\)
  • Q4: \(f\) odd, \(g\) even, \((f\circ g)(x)=(x^2+1)^3-(x^2+1)\)
  • Q5: \(x=-2,8\)
  • Q6: Vertex \((2,-3)\), x-intercepts \((-1,0)\), \((5,0)\), range \(y\ge -3\)

Day 4

  • Q1: Domain \(x>1\), solution \(x=\frac{10}{9}\)
  • Q2: \(x=1\)
  • Q3: \((x-2)^2+(y+3)^2=4\), centre \((2,-3)\), radius \(2\)
  • Q4: Even
  • Q5: \(x\le -1\) or \(x\ge 4\)
  • Q6: Vertex \((1,-4)\), axis \(x=1\), x-intercepts \((-1,0),(3,0)\), y-int \((0,-3)\)
  • Q7: \(D=b^2-4ac=36-52=-16<0\), no real roots

Day 5

  • Q1: \(x=2\)
  • Q2: domain \(\mathbb{R}\), range \((0,\infty)\), asymptote \(y=0\)
  • Q3: Domain \([-5,1]\), range \([-2,4]\)
  • Q4: \((f\circ g)(x)=|x-2|\), equals 0 at \(x=2\)
  • Q5: Double real root at \(x=2\), vertex \((2,0)\)
  • Q6: Centre \((3,2)\), asymptotes \(x=3,y=2\), x-int \((1,0)\), y-int \((0,\frac23)\), domain \(x\neq3\), range \(y\neq2\)
  • Q7: \(m=-\frac12\), equation \(x+2y-5=0\), intercepts \((5,0)\), \((0,\frac52)\)
  • Q8: parallel slope \(\frac37\), equation \(3x-7y+15=0\)
