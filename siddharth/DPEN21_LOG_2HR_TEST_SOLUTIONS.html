Complete worked answers with labelled sketches/diagrams for all graphing questions.

Section A Solutions

Q1

\(\log_3 27=3,\ \log_2 128=7,\ \log_4 0.25=-1\).

Q2

  • \(\log_2 16+\log_2 8=\log_2(128)=7\)
  • \(\log 2+\log 5=\log 10=1\)
  • \(\log_2 18-2\log_2 3=\log_2(18)-\log_2(9)=\log_2 2=1\)

Q3

  • \(\log_2 16+\log_2 x=\log_2 32\Rightarrow 4+\log_2 x=5\Rightarrow x=2\)
  • \(e^x=25\Rightarrow x=\ln 25\)

Q4

  • \(f(x)=x^3-x\Rightarrow f(-x)=-f(x)\): odd
  • \(g(x)=x^2+1\Rightarrow g(-x)=g(x)\): even
  • \((f\circ g)(x)=f(x^2+1)=(x^2+1)^3-(x^2+1)\)

Q5

  • Vertex of \(h(x)=|x-2|-3\): \((2,-3)\)
  • x-intercepts: \(|x-2|=3\Rightarrow x=-1,5\)
  • Range: \(y\ge -3\)

Section C Solutions

Q12

  • \(2x-5\le 9\Rightarrow x\le 7\)
  • \(-3<2x+1\le 7\Rightarrow -4<2x\le 6\Rightarrow -2<x\le 3\)

Q13

\(|2x-3|\ge 5\Rightarrow 2x-3\ge 5\) or \(2x-3\le -5\). So \(x\ge 4\) or \(x\le -1\).

Q14

Domain: \(x>1\). \( \log x-\log(x-1)=1\Rightarrow \log\!\left(\frac{x}{x-1}\right)=1\Rightarrow \frac{x}{x-1}=10\Rightarrow x=\frac{10}{9}\), valid since \(x>1\).

Q15

\(2^{x+1}=4^{3x-2}=(2^2)^{3x-2}=2^{6x-4}\Rightarrow x+1=6x-4\Rightarrow x=1\).

Q16

\((f\circ g)(x)=f(x-2)=|x-2|\). It equals 0 when \(x-2=0\Rightarrow x=2\).
