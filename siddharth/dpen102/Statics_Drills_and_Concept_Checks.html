Warm-Up Drills & Concept Checks

A companion to your 20-Week Full Problem Set. Use this first, every week, before the main problem. Short, numeric, no story problems to parse, just reps.

Suggested weekly flow: Drills (≈10 min, 4 quick numeric reps) → Concept Check (≈5 min, 3 short answers) → Main Problem from the Full Problem Set (≈90 min) → check both answer keys (≈10 min). If a week feels like too much, drills + concepts alone still count as a real session, don't skip the whole week over it. Something beats nothing.

  3. Pushes them apart.
  4. Yes, always, that's the action-reaction check.
Concept Check

  2. Why isolate each member of a frame separately, rather than treating the whole frame as one rigid body?
