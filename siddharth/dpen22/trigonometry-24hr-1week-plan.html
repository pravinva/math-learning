Daily Plan (Exact Mapping)

Target per day: 3.5 hours on Days 1-6, then 3 hours on Day 7.
Day Hours Topic Mapping Focus Question Target
Day 1 3.5h Topic 1 Trig ratios, exact values, 30-45-60 triangles, reciprocal ratios 8-10 questions
Day 2 3.5h Topic 2 Radians, unit circle, CAST, coterminal angles, arc/sector formulas 8-10 questions
Day 3 3.5h Topic 3 Trig graph transformations (\(a,b,c,d\)), period/amplitude/phase 8-10 questions
Day 4 3.5h Topic 4 Trig equations in ranges, exact-value solving, general solutions 8-10 questions
Day 5 3.5h Topic 5 Identities, simplification, proof structure, algebraic manipulation 8-10 questions
Day 6 3.5h Topics 6-7 Sine/cosine rules, area formula, compound & double-angle methods 10-12 questions
Day 7 3h Topic 8 + Exam Practice Inverse trig, mixed 20-question set, timed exam blocks, error fixes 10-15 mixed + review
Total question target for the week: 55-75 with full written steps for hard identity/equation questions.

Coverage

  • Graph parameter extraction is reliable
  • Identity proofs use clear valid transformations

Performance

  • At least 55 mixed trig questions solved
  • 2 timed sets (45-60 mins each)
  • 1-page formula + strategy sheet finalised
  • Error log reviewed and weak items re-done
