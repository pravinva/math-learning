NSW Year 12 Extension 1 Calculus

Published as a DPEN22 revision companion. This page helps you use the PDF strategically for university readiness.

Alignment summary: This resource is mainly DPEN22 / Calc 1 level, with some topics that bridge into MATH141 readiness. It is not at full MATH283 depth.

Core topics covered in the PDF

  1. Integration by substitution
  2. Trigonometric integration identities
  3. Derivatives of inverse trigonometric functions
  4. Integrals that give inverse trigonometric functions
  5. Volumes of solids of revolution

How to use this for DPEN22

  • Use substitution + trig integration sets as speed drills after DPEN22 class topics.
  • Use inverse-trig derivative/integral sections as extension practice after core DPEN22 tests.
  • Use solids-of-revolution questions to strengthen setup discipline and bounds selection.
  • Attempt mixed sections under timed conditions, then compare with worked solutions.

Bridge to MATH141

  • Good for fluency in symbolic calculus operations and exam pacing.
  • Useful pre-work before multivariable/vector/matrix-heavy MATH141 components.
  • Follow with MATH141 preparedness packs in this DPEN22 section.
