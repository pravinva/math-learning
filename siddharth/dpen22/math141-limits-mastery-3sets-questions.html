Set A - Core Limit Fluency

  1. \(\lim_{x\to2}\dfrac{x^2-4}{x-2}\)
  2. \(\lim_{x\to3}\dfrac{x^2-9}{x-3}\)
  3. \(\lim_{x\to1}\dfrac{x^3-1}{x-1}\)
  4. \(\lim_{x\to0}\dfrac{\sin(4x)}{x}\)
  5. \(\lim_{x\to0}\dfrac{\tan x}{x}\)
  6. \(\lim_{x\to0}\dfrac{1-\cos x}{x^2}\)
  7. \(\lim_{x\to\infty}\dfrac{5x^2-3x+1}{2x^2+7}\)
  8. \(\lim_{x\to\infty}\dfrac{4x^3+1}{x^3-2x}\)
  9. \(\lim_{x\to-\infty}\dfrac{3x^2-1}{x^2+9}\)
  10. \(\lim_{x\to\infty}\dfrac{\sqrt{x^2+5x}}{x}\)
  11. \(\lim_{x\to\infty}\left(\sqrt{x^2+2x}-x\right)\)
  12. \(\lim_{x\to2^-}\dfrac{|x-2|}{x-2}\)
  13. \(\lim_{x\to2^+}\dfrac{|x-2|}{x-2}\)
  14. \(\lim_{x\to2}\dfrac{|x-2|}{x-2}\) (if exists)
  15. \(\lim_{x\to0}\dfrac{e^{3x}-1}{x}\)
  16. \(\lim_{x\to0}\dfrac{\ln(1+x)}{x}\)
  17. \(\lim_{x\to0}\dfrac{\sin x - x}{x^3}\)
  18. \(\lim_{x\to0}\dfrac{e^x-1-x}{x^2}\)
  19. Check continuity at \(x=1\): \(f(x)=\begin{cases}x^2,&x<1\\2x-1,&x\ge1\end{cases}\)
  20. Check continuity at \(x=0\): \(g(x)=\begin{cases}\dfrac{\sin x}{x},&x\ne0\\1,&x=0\end{cases}\)

Set B - One-Sided + Piecewise + Infinity Behavior

  1. \(\lim_{x\to-1}\dfrac{x^2-1}{x+1}\)
  2. \(\lim_{x\to4}\dfrac{\sqrt{x}-2}{x-4}\)
  3. \(\lim_{x\to0}\dfrac{\sin(7x)}{2x}\)
  4. \(\lim_{x\to0}\dfrac{\tan(5x)}{x}\)
  5. \(\lim_{x\to0}\dfrac{1-\cos(3x)}{x^2}\)
  6. \(\lim_{x\to\infty}\dfrac{6x-1}{\sqrt{9x^2+2}}\)
  7. \(\lim_{x\to-\infty}\dfrac{2x}{\sqrt{x^2+4}}\)
  8. \(\lim_{x\to\infty}\left(\sqrt{x^2+8x}-x\right)\)
  9. \(\lim_{x\to\infty}\left(\sqrt{4x^2+x}-2x\right)\)
  10. \(\lim_{x\to1^-}\dfrac{1}{x-1}\)
  11. \(\lim_{x\to1^+}\dfrac{1}{x-1}\)
  12. \(\lim_{x\to1}\dfrac{1}{(x-1)^2}\)
  13. \(\lim_{x\to0}\dfrac{a^x-1}{x}\) in terms of \(a>0\), \(a\ne1\)
  14. \(\lim_{x\to0}\dfrac{\ln(1+2x)}{x}\)
  15. \(\lim_{x\to0}\dfrac{\sin(2x)-2x}{x^3}\)
  16. \(\lim_{x\to0}\dfrac{\cos x-1+\frac{x^2}{2}}{x^4}\)
  17. Find \(k\) for continuity at \(x=2\): \(f(x)=\begin{cases}x^2+k,&x<2\\3x,&x\ge2\end{cases}\)
  18. Find \(a,b\) so \(f(x)=\begin{cases}ax+b,&x<1\\x^2,&x\ge1\end{cases}\) is continuous at \(x=1\) and \(f(0)=2\)
  19. State all vertical asymptotes of \(f(x)=\dfrac{x+1}{x^2-4}\)
  20. State horizontal asymptote of \(f(x)=\dfrac{3x^2-5}{x^2+1}\)

Set C - Challenge Mix

  1. \(\lim_{x\to0}\dfrac{\sqrt{1+3x}-1}{x}\)
  2. \(\lim_{x\to0}\dfrac{\sqrt{1+x}-\sqrt{1-x}}{x}\)
  3. \(\lim_{x\to0}\dfrac{\ln(1+x)-x}{x^2}\)
  4. \(\lim_{x\to0}\dfrac{e^{2x}-1-2x}{x^2}\)
  5. \(\lim_{x\to0}\dfrac{\sin(3x)-3x}{x^3}\)
  6. \(\lim_{x\to0}\dfrac{\tan x-\sin x}{x^3}\)
  7. \(\lim_{x\to\infty}\dfrac{x^2+3x}{2x^2-5}\)
  8. \(\lim_{x\to\infty}\dfrac{5x^3-1}{\sqrt{25x^6+x}}\)
  9. \(\lim_{x\to-\infty}\dfrac{4x^3+7}{\sqrt{16x^6+1}}\)
  10. \(\lim_{x\to\infty}\left(\sqrt{x^2+10}-x\right)\)
  11. \(\lim_{x\to0^+}\ln x\)
  12. \(\lim_{x\to\infty}\ln x\)
  13. \(\lim_{x\to-\infty}e^x\)
  14. \(\lim_{x\to\infty}xe^{-x}\)
  15. \(\lim_{x\to0}\dfrac{|x|}{x}\) (if exists)
  16. \(\lim_{x\to0}\dfrac{x}{|x|}\) (if exists)
  17. Is \(f(x)=\begin{cases}\dfrac{x^2-1}{x-1},&x\ne1\\2,&x=1\end{cases}\) continuous at \(x=1\)?
  18. For \(f(x)=\dfrac{x^2-1}{x-1}\), define \(f(1)\) to remove discontinuity.
  19. Find \(\lim_{x\to2}\dfrac{x^3-8}{x-2}\).
  20. Find \(\lim_{x\to0}\dfrac{(1+x)^5-1}{x}\).
