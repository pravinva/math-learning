Set A - Foundations + Fluency

  1. Evaluate \(\lim_{x\to2}\dfrac{x^2-4}{x-2}\). limits
  2. Evaluate \(\lim_{x\to0}\dfrac{\sin(5x)}{x}\).
  3. Evaluate \(\lim_{x\to\infty}\dfrac{4x^2-3x+1}{2x^2+7}\).
  4. Find domain of \(f(x)=\ln(x-2)\).
  5. Differentiate \(y=(x^3-2x)e^x\).
  6. Differentiate \(y=\dfrac{2x+1}{x-3}\).
  7. Differentiate \(y=\ln(3x^2+1)\).
  8. Differentiate \(y=\log_{10}(x^2+4)\).
  9. Differentiate \(y=\arcsin(2x)\).
  10. Differentiate \(y=\arctan(3x-1)\).
  11. Find equation of tangent to \(y=x^3-4x\) at \(x=2\).
  12. For \(f(x)=x^3-3x^2-9x+2\), find stationary points.
  13. For \(g(x)=x^4-4x^3\), find intervals of concavity.
  14. Differentiate implicitly: \(x^2+y^2=25\).
  15. Differentiate implicitly: \(xy+y^2=6\).
  16. Given \(x=t^2+1,\ y=t^3-2t\), find \(\dfrac{dy}{dx}\).
  17. Given \(x=\cos t,\ y=\sin t\), find \(\dfrac{d^2y}{dx^2}\) at \(t=\pi/4\).
  18. Differentiate \(y=\sinh(2x)\).
  19. Differentiate \(y=\cosh(3x-1)\).
  20. Solve \(e^{2x}=7\) for \(x\).

Set B - Mixed Skills + Readiness Depth

  1. Evaluate \(\lim_{x\to1}\dfrac{x^3-1}{x-1}\). limits
  2. Evaluate \(\lim_{x\to0}\dfrac{1-\cos x}{x^2}\).
  3. Evaluate \(\lim_{x\to\infty}\left(\sqrt{x^2+5x}-x\right)\).
  4. Find domain of \(f(x)=\sqrt{5-2x}\).
  5. Differentiate \(y=x^2\ln x\).
  6. Differentiate \(y=e^{x^2}(x-1)\).
  7. Differentiate \(y=\ln\!\left(\dfrac{x+1}{x-1}\right)\).
  8. Differentiate \(y=(\arctan x)^2\).
  9. Differentiate \(y=\operatorname{arccos}(x/2)\).
  10. Find \(f'(1)\) if \(f(x)=\dfrac{x^2+1}{x+2}\).
  11. For \(f(x)=2x^3-15x^2+24x\), find and classify stationary points.
  12. Find inflection point(s) of \(g(x)=x^3-6x^2+9x+1\).
  13. Find where \(h(x)=x^3-9x\) is increasing/decreasing.
  14. Differentiate implicitly: \(x^2y+y^3=10\).
  15. Differentiate implicitly: \(\sin(xy)=x+y\).
  16. For \(x=t^3,\ y=t^2+t\), find \(\dfrac{dy}{dx}\).
  17. For \(x=2\cos t,\ y=2\sin t\), find tangent slope at \(t=\pi/3\).
  18. Differentiate \(y=\tanh(4x)\).
  19. Solve \(\ln(2x-1)=3\) for \(x\).
  20. Solve \(3^{x+1}=20\) for \(x\) (exact logarithmic form).

Set C - Exam-Style Bridge Pack

  1. Evaluate \(\lim_{x\to3}\dfrac{x^2-9}{x-3}\). limits
  2. Evaluate \(\lim_{x\to0}\dfrac{\tan x}{x}\).
  3. Evaluate \(\lim_{x\to\infty}\dfrac{5x^3-1}{2x^3+7x}\).
  4. Find domain of \(f(x)=\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{x-4}}\).
  5. Differentiate \(y=(x^2+1)\ln x\).
  6. Differentiate \(y=\dfrac{e^x}{x^2+1}\).
  7. Differentiate \(y=\ln(\sin x)\).
  8. Differentiate \(y=\operatorname{arcsin}(x^2)\).
  9. Differentiate \(y=\operatorname{arccot}(2x)\) using \(d(\arccot u)/dx=-u'/(1+u^2)\).
  10. Find normal line to \(y=x^2\) at \(x=1\).
  11. For \(f(x)=x^3-6x^2+12x+1\), find stationary point(s).
  12. Find inflection point(s) of \(g(x)=x^4-2x^3+x\).
  13. Find intervals where \(p(x)=x^3-3x^2-24x\) is concave up/down.
  14. Differentiate implicitly: \(x^2+xy+y^2=7\).
  15. Differentiate implicitly: \(e^{xy}=x+y\).
  16. Given \(x=t-\sin t,\ y=1-\cos t\), find \(\dfrac{dy}{dx}\).
  17. Given \(x=t^2,\ y=t^4-3t\), find \(\dfrac{d^2y}{dx^2}\).
  18. Differentiate \(y=\operatorname{sech}(x)\).
  19. Solve \(\cosh x=3\) for \(x\) (inverse hyperbolic form).
  20. Solve \(e^x+e^{-x}=5\) for \(x\).
