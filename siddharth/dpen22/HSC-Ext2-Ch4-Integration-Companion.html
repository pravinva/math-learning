HSC Extension 2 - Chapter 4 Integration (DPEN22 Companion)

This page pulls the Chapter 4 integration content into the DPEN22 revision section and maps each part to DPEN22/MATH141 alignment.

Short answer: Chapter 4 is not all pure Calc 1. It is mostly Calc 1 / Calc 1+ integration fluency, with some advanced-algebraic depth (especially recurrence relations).

How to use this under DPEN22

  1. Start with 4.1, 4.2, and 4.10 to lock DPEN22 integration fundamentals.
  2. Then use 4.3, 4.4, and 4.8 for MATH141-prep extension.
  3. Treat 4.6 and 4.7 as challenge topics once trig fluency is solid.
  4. Leave 4.9 (recurrence) as advanced enrichment, not core DPEN22 requirement.
Practical alignment summary:
DPEN22-compatible core: about 60-70% of Chapter 4.
MATH141/MATH142 bridge + advanced extension: about 30-40%.
