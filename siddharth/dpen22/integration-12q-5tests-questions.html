How to think through Q8-Q12 style integration problems
1) Identify what the integral represents: area, displacement, total quantity, average value, work, or volume.
2) Write a clean integrand in ONE variable: substitute first if needed, then integrate.
3) Set realistic bounds: use the interval from the question and check physical meaning.
4) For area between curves: use top minus bottom and split intervals if curves cross.
5) For total distance: integrate \(|v(t)|\), not just \(v(t)\), when velocity changes sign.
6) For average value: use \(\dfrac{1}{b-a}\int_a^b f(x)\,dx\).
7) State final answer with units: include interpretation in one sentence.
8) Quick check: does your result make sense in size/sign for the context?

Test 1

  1. Evaluate \(\int (6x^2-8x+3)\,dx\).
  2. Evaluate \(\int (2x-1)^5\,dx\).
  3. Evaluate \(\int x e^x\,dx\).
  4. Evaluate \(\int \dfrac{3x+2}{x-4}\,dx\).
  5. Evaluate \(\int_0^2 (5x^2+1)\,dx\).
  6. Evaluate \(\int_0^{\pi/3}\sin(3x)\,dx\).
  7. Evaluate \(\int x\ln x\,dx\).
  8. On a design sketch, the upper boundary of a panel is \(y=2x\) and the lower boundary is \(y=x^2\) for \(0\le x\le2\). Find the exact enclosed area.
  9. A lift cart moves along a straight shaft with velocity \(v(t)=3t^2-12t+9\) m/s for \(0\le t\le5\) s. Find (i) net displacement and (ii) total distance travelled.
  10. For a manufactured part, marginal cost is modeled by \(C'(q)=3q^2-18q+24\) dollars per unit, where \(q\) is units produced and \(C(0)=40\). Find the cost function \(C(q)\), then evaluate \(C(4)\).
  11. A tank is filled by a pump with flow rate \(r(t)=2t+1\) L/min over \(0\le t\le10\) min. Find the total volume delivered and the average flow rate on this interval.
  12. A decorative arch is described by \(y=12-x^2\). Determine the total area enclosed between the curve and the \(x\)-axis.

Test 2

  1. Evaluate \(\int \left(15x^2+\dfrac{4}{x^2}\right)\,dx\).
  2. Evaluate \(\int 4(2x-3)(x^2-3x+1)^3\,dx\).
  3. Evaluate \(\int \dfrac{-8x}{(x^2-2)^2}\,dx\).
  4. Evaluate \(\int e^{2x}(2\cos x-\sin x)\,dx\).
  5. Evaluate \(\int \dfrac{1-\ln x}{x^2}\,dx\).
  6. Evaluate \(\int 4\sin(4x)\cos(4x)\,dx\).
  7. Evaluate \(\int_0^6 |x-3|\,dx\).
  8. In a cross-section, two boundary curves are \(y=x+2\) and \(y=x^2\) over \([-1,2]\). Find the full enclosed area between the curves.
  9. A moving cart has acceleration \(a(t)=6t-4\) m/s\(^2\), and initial velocity \(v(0)=3\) m/s. Determine \(v(t)\), then find displacement from \(t=0\) to \(t=4\) s.
  10. A company models marginal profit by \(P'(q)=-3q^2+24q-36\), with \(P(0)=20\). Find the profit function \(P(q)\), then compute profit at \(q=6\).
  11. The response of a system is \(f(x)=x^3-3x\) for \(x\in[-1,2]\). Find the average value of the response across this interval.
  12. The load profile of a beam is \(w(x)=kx+2\) over \(0\le x\le3\), and total load is 24 units. Find the value of \(k\).
