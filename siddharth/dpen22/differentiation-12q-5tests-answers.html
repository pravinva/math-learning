Answer Key — Test 1

  1. \(f'(x)=12x^3-10x+7\)
  2. \(y'=10(2x-1)^4\)
  3. \(y'=e^x(x+1)^2\)
  4. \(y'=-\dfrac{14}{(x-4)^2}\)
  5. \(y'=\dfrac{10x}{5x^2+1}\)
  6. \(y'=e^{-x}(3\cos 3x-\sin 3x)\)
  7. At \(x=1\): local max \((1,5)\). At \(x=3\): local min \((3,1)\).
  8. Concave up: \((-\infty,0)\cup(2,\infty)\); concave down: \((0,2)\). Inflection points: \((0,2)\), \((2,-14)\).
  9. Q9 full steps:
    \(C(q)=q^3-9q^2+24q+40\).
    \(C'(q)=3q^2-18q+24=3(q-2)(q-4)\). Critical points: \(q=2,4\).
    Sign of \(C'\): positive for \(q\lt2\), negative for \(2\lt q\lt4\), positive for \(q\gt4\).
    So increasing on \((0,2)\cup(4,\infty)\), decreasing on \((2,4)\).
    \(C(2)=8-36+48+40=60\Rightarrow\) local max \((2,60)\).
    \(C(4)=64-144+96+40=56\Rightarrow\) local min \((4,56)\).
  10. Q10 full steps:
    \(h(t)=-t^3+6t^2-9t+5\).
    \(h'(t)=-3t^2+12t-9=-3(t-1)(t-3)\Rightarrow t=1,3\).
    Sign of \(h'\): negative on \((0,1)\), positive on \((1,3)\), negative on \((3,5)\).
    Hence falling, rising, then falling.
    \(h(1)=-1+6-9+5=1\Rightarrow\) local min \((1,1)\).
    \(h(3)=-27+54-27+5=5\Rightarrow\) local max \((3,5)\).
    \(h''(t)=-6t+12\). Set \(h''=0\Rightarrow t=2\). Concavity changes there, and \(h(2)=3\). Inflection at \((2,3)\).
  11. Q11 full steps:
    Let width \(w\) (perpendicular to wall), length \(L\) (parallel to wall).
    Fencing: \(L+2w=200\Rightarrow L=200-2w\).
    Area: \(A(w)=w(200-2w)=200w-2w^2\).
    \(A'(w)=200-4w=0\Rightarrow w=50\text{ m}\).
    \(A''(w)=-4\lt0\Rightarrow\) maximum area.
    \(L=200-2(50)=100\text{ m}\).
    \(A_{\max}=100\times50=5000\text{ m}^2\).
  12. Q12 full steps:
    \(V(x)=x(30-2x)(20-2x)=4x^3-100x^2+600x,\quad 0\lt x\lt10\).
    \(V'(x)=12x^2-200x+600=4(3x^2-50x+150)\).
    Set \(V'=0\): \(3x^2-50x+150=0\Rightarrow x=\dfrac{25\pm5\sqrt7}{3}\).
    Only feasible root in \((0,10)\): \(x=\dfrac{25-5\sqrt7}{3}\approx3.92\text{ cm}\).
    \(V''(x)=24x-200\), and at \(x\approx3.92\), \(V''\lt0\Rightarrow\) maximum.
    \(V_{\max}\approx1056\text{ cm}^3\).

Answer Key — Test 2

  1. \(f'(x)=15x^2+\dfrac{4}{x^2}\)
  2. \(y'=4(2x-3)(x^2-3x+1)^3\)
  3. \(y'=-\dfrac{8x}{(x^2-2)^2}\)
  4. \(y'=e^{2x}(2\cos x-\sin x)\)
  5. \(y'=\dfrac{1-\ln x}{x^2}\)
  6. \(y'=8\sin(4x)\cos(4x)=4\sin(8x)\)
  7. Local max \((2,28)\), local min \((3,27)\).
  8. Concave down for \(x\lt 1\), concave up for \(x\gt 1\). Inflection at \((1,-21)\).
  9. Q9 full steps:
    \(y=x^3-3x^2-9x+27\).
    \(y'=3x^2-6x-9=3(x-3)(x+1)\Rightarrow x=-1,3\).
    Sign of \(y'\): \(+\) then \(-\) then \(+\), so local max at \(x=-1\), local min at \(x=3\).
    \(y(-1)=32,\ y(3)=0\).
    \(y''=6x-6\). Set \(y''=0\Rightarrow x=1\); concavity changes across \(x=1\).
    Inflection point: \(y(1)=16\Rightarrow(1,16)\).
  10. Q10 full steps:
    \(P(q)=-q^3+12q^2-36q+20\).
    \(P'(q)=-3q^2+24q-36=-3(q-2)(q-6)\Rightarrow q=2,6\).
    Sign of \(P'\): negative on \((0,2)\), positive on \((2,6)\), negative on \((6,10)\).
    So decreasing, increasing, decreasing.
    \(P(2)=-12\Rightarrow\) local min \((2,-12)\).
    \(P(6)=20\Rightarrow\) local max \((6,20)\).
    \(P''(q)=-6q+24\Rightarrow q=4\) is inflection; \(P(4)=4\Rightarrow(4,4)\).
  11. Q11 full steps:
    Volume constraint: \(\pi r^2h=500\Rightarrow h=\dfrac{500}{\pi r^2}\).
    Surface area (closed cylinder): \(S=2\pi r^2+2\pi rh\).
    Substitute \(h\): \(S(r)=2\pi r^2+\dfrac{1000}{r}\).
    \(S'(r)=4\pi r-\dfrac{1000}{r^2}=0\Rightarrow4\pi r^3=1000\Rightarrow r^3=\dfrac{250}{\pi}\).
    \(\displaystyle r=\left(\dfrac{250}{\pi}\right)^{1/3}\). Then \(\displaystyle h=\frac{500}{\pi r^2}=2r\).
    (And \(S''(r)=4\pi+\dfrac{2000}{r^3}\gt0\), so minimum.)
  12. Q12 full steps:
    Let numbers be \(x\) and \(40-x\), with \(0\le x\le40\).
    Product \(P(x)=x(40-x)=40x-x^2\).
    \(P'(x)=40-2x=0\Rightarrow x=20\).
    \(P''(x)=-2\lt0\Rightarrow\) maximum.
    Numbers: \(20,20\). Maximum product: \(400\).

Answer Key — Test 3

  1. \(y'=6x(x^2+4)^2\)
  2. \(y'=e^{-x}(-x^2+2x+1)\)
  3. \(y'=\dfrac{-2x^2-2x+2}{(x^2+1)^2}\)
  4. \(y'=\dfrac{6x-2}{3x^2-2x+5}\)
  5. \(y'=-10x\sin(5x^2)\)
  6. \(y'=\ln x+1\)
  7. Local max \((0,3)\); local mins \((-2,-13)\), \((2,-13)\).
  8. Concave down for \(x\lt -1\), concave up for \(x\gt -1\). Inflection at \((-1,27)\).
  9. Q9 full steps:
    \(T(t)=t^3-12t+20\), \(0\le t\le5\).
    \(T'(t)=3t^2-12=3(t-2)(t+2)\). In domain, only critical point is \(t=2\).
    Sign: \(T'(t)\lt0\) on \((0,2)\), \(T'(t)\gt0\) on \((2,5)\).
    So decreasing then increasing; local min at \(t=2\).
    \(T(2)=8-24+20=4\Rightarrow(2,4)\).
    \(T''(t)=6t\gt0\) for \(t\in(0,5)\), so concave up on the whole interval.
  10. Q10 full steps:
    \(F(x)=x^3-6x^2+9x+15\).
    \(F'(x)=3x^2-12x+9=3(x-1)(x-3)\Rightarrow x=1,3\).
    Sign of \(F'\): \(+\) on \((0,1)\), \(-\) on \((1,3)\), \(+\) on \((3,5)\).
    So local max at \(x=1\), local min at \(x=3\).
    \(F(1)=19,\ F(3)=15\).
    \(F''(x)=6x-12\Rightarrow x=2\) inflection. \(F(2)=17\).
    Concave down for \(x\lt2\), concave up for \(x\gt2\).
  11. Q11 full steps:
    Let sides be \(x\) and \(y\), with \(2x+2y=120\Rightarrow y=60-x\).
    Area \(A(x)=x(60-x)=60x-x^2\).
    \(A'(x)=60-2x=0\Rightarrow x=30\), so \(y=30\).
    \(A''(x)=-2\lt0\Rightarrow\) maximum area.
    Maximum area \(=30\times30=900\text{ m}^2\).
  12. Q12 full steps:
    Volume: \(x^2h=256\Rightarrow h=\dfrac{256}{x^2}\).
    Open-top material area: \(S=x^2+4xh=x^2+\dfrac{1024}{x}\).
    \(S'(x)=2x-\dfrac{1024}{x^2}=0\Rightarrow2x^3=1024\Rightarrow x^3=512\Rightarrow x=8\).
    \(h=\dfrac{256}{8^2}=4\).
    \(S''(x)=2+\dfrac{2048}{x^3}\gt0\), so this is a minimum-material design.

Answer Key — Test 4

  1. \(y'=6x^2(x^3-1)\)
  2. \(y'=\dfrac{x\cos x-\sin x}{x^2}\)
  3. \(y'=2x\ln x+x+\dfrac1x\)
  4. \(y'=3e^{3x+1}\)
  5. \(y'=\cot x\)
  6. \(y'=-15(5x-2)^{-4}\)
  7. Local min \((1,0)\), local max \((3,4)\).
  8. \(g''(x)=12(x-1)^2\ge 0\): concave up for all \(x\), no inflection point.
  9. Q9 full steps:
    \(R(p)=p^3-9p^2+24p\), \(0\le p\le8\).
    \(R'(p)=3p^2-18p+24=3(p-2)(p-4)\Rightarrow p=2,4\).
    Sign of \(R'\): \(+\) on \((0,2)\), \(-\) on \((2,4)\), \(+\) on \((4,8)\).
    Local max at \(p=2\): \(R(2)=20\). Local min at \(p=4\): \(R(4)=16\).
    \(R''(p)=6p-18\Rightarrow p=3\) inflection; \(R(3)=18\).
  10. Q10 full steps:
    \(s(t)=2t^3-9t^2+12t+1\), \(0\le t\le4\).
    \(s'(t)=6t^2-18t+12=6(t-1)(t-2)\Rightarrow t=1,2\).
    Sign of \(s'\): \(+\) on \((0,1)\), \(-\) on \((1,2)\), \(+\) on \((2,4)\).
    Thus local max at \(t=1\), local min at \(t=2\).
    \(s(1)=6,\ s(2)=5\).
    \(s''(t)=12t-18\Rightarrow t=1.5\) concavity transition (inflection).
    \(s(1.5)=5.5\).
  11. Q11 full steps:
    Let rectangle sides \(x,y\). Perimeter fixed: \(2x+2y=100\Rightarrow y=50-x\).
    Area \(A(x)=x(50-x)=50x-x^2\).
    \(A'(x)=50-2x=0\Rightarrow x=25\), hence \(y=25\).
    \(A''(x)=-2\lt0\Rightarrow\) maximum area.
    Maximum area \(=25\times25=625\text{ m}^2\).
  12. Q12 full steps:
    Volume constraint (open-top square base): \(x^2h=500\Rightarrow h=\dfrac{500}{x^2}\).
    Surface area (no lid): \(S=x^2+4xh=x^2+\dfrac{2000}{x}\).
    \(S'(x)=2x-\dfrac{2000}{x^2}=0\Rightarrow2x^3=2000\Rightarrow x^3=1000\Rightarrow x=10\).
    \(h=\dfrac{500}{10^2}=5\).
    \(S''(x)=2+\dfrac{4000}{x^3}\gt0\), so minimum area at \(x=10,h=5\).

Answer Key — Test 5

  1. \(y'=3(8x-3)(4x^2-3x+1)^2\)
  2. \(y'=\dfrac{e^x(x^2+x+1)}{(x+1)^2}\)
  3. \(y'=\dfrac{2}{x(x^2+1)}-\dfrac{2\ln(x^2+1)}{x^3}\)
  4. \(y'=2\sec^2(2x-1)\)
  5. \(y'=e^{-2x}(\cos x-2\sin x)\)
  6. \(y'=\dfrac{2x+3}{2\sqrt{x^2+3x+2}}\)
  7. Local max \((-1,10)\), local min \((3,-22)\).
  8. Concave down on \(\left(-\dfrac1{\sqrt3},\dfrac1{\sqrt3}\right)\), concave up outside. Inflection points \(\left(\pm\dfrac1{\sqrt3},\dfrac{58}{9}\right)\).
  9. Q9 full steps:
    \(C(t)=-t^3+9t^2-24t+30\), \(0\le t\le8\).
    \(C'(t)=-3t^2+18t-24=-3(t-2)(t-4)\Rightarrow t=2,4\).
    Sign of \(C'\): \(-\) on \((0,2)\), \(+\) on \((2,4)\), \(-\) on \((4,8)\).
    So local min at \(t=2\), local max at \(t=4\).
    \(C(2)=10,\ C(4)=14\).
    \(C''(t)=-6t+18\Rightarrow t=3\) inflection; \(C(3)=12\).
  10. Q10 full steps:
    \(y=x^3-6x\), \(-3\le x\le3\).
    \(y'=3x^2-6=3(x^2-2)\Rightarrow x=\pm\sqrt2\).
    \(y''=6x\). At \(x=-\sqrt2\), \(y''\lt0\Rightarrow\) local max. At \(x=\sqrt2\), \(y''\gt0\Rightarrow\) local min.
    \(y(-\sqrt2)=4\sqrt2,\ y(\sqrt2)=-4\sqrt2\).
    \(y''=0\) at \(x=0\) and sign changes there \(\Rightarrow\) inflection \((0,0)\).
    Concave down for \(x\lt0\), concave up for \(x\gt0\).
  11. Q11 full steps:
    Fixed area: \(xy=400\Rightarrow y=\dfrac{400}{x}\).
    Perimeter \(P=2x+2y=2x+\dfrac{800}{x}\).
    \(P'(x)=2-\dfrac{800}{x^2}=0\Rightarrow x^2=400\Rightarrow x=20\) (positive length).
    \(y=\dfrac{400}{20}=20\).
    \(P_{\min}=2(20)+2(20)=80\text{ m}\).
    (Also \(P''(x)=\dfrac{1600}{x^3}\gt0\), confirming minimum.)
  12. Q12 full steps:
    \(V(x)=x(24-2x)^2,\ 0\lt x\lt12\).
    Expand: \(V=4x^3-96x^2+576x\).
    \(V'=12x^2-192x+576=12(x^2-16x+48)\).
    Critical points: \(x=\dfrac{16\pm\sqrt{256-192}}{2}=4,12\).
    \(x=12\) gives zero volume at boundary, so interior optimum is \(x=4\).
    \(V(4)=4(16)^2=1024\text{ cm}^3\) (maximum).
