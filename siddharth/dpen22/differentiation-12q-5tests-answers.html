Answer Key — Test 1

  1. \(f'(x)=12x^3-10x+7\)
  2. \(y'=10(2x-1)^4\)
  3. \(y'=e^x(x+1)^2\)
  4. \(y'=-\dfrac{14}{(x-4)^2}\)
  5. \(y'=\dfrac{10x}{5x^2+1}\)
  6. \(y'=e^{-x}(3\cos 3x-\sin 3x)\)
  7. At \(x=1\): local max \((1,5)\). At \(x=3\): local min \((3,1)\).
  8. Concave up: \((-\infty,0)\cup(2,\infty)\); concave down: \((0,2)\). Inflection points: \((0,2)\), \((2,-14)\).
  9. Increasing on \((0,2)\cup(4,\infty)\), decreasing on \((2,4)\). Local max \((2,60)\), local min \((4,56)\).
  10. Rising on \((1,3)\); falling on \((0,1)\cup(3,5)\). Local min \((1,1)\), local max \((3,5)\). Inflection at \((2,3)\).
  11. Width \(50\text{ m}\), length \(100\text{ m}\), maximum area \(5000\text{ m}^2\).
  12. \(x=\dfrac{25-5\sqrt7}{3}\approx3.92\text{ cm}\), \(V_{\max}\approx1056\text{ cm}^3\).

Answer Key — Test 2

  2. \(y'=4(2x-3)(x^2-3x+1)^3\)
  3. \(y'=-\dfrac{8x}{(x^2-2)^2}\)
  4. \(y'=e^{2x}(2\cos x-\sin x)\)
  5. \(y'=\dfrac{1-\ln x}{x^2}\)
  6. \(y'=8\sin(4x)\cos(4x)=4\sin(8x)\)
  7. Local max \((2,28)\), local min \((3,27)\).
  8. Concave down for \(x\lt 1\), concave up for \(x\gt 1\). Inflection at \((1,-21)\).
  9. Local max at \((-1,32)\), local min at \((3,0)\), inflection at \((1,16)\).
  10. Increasing on \((2,6)\), decreasing on \((0,2)\cup(6,10)\). Local min \((2,-12)\), local max \((6,20)\), inflection \((4,4)\).
  11. \(r=\left(\dfrac{250}{\pi}\right)^{1/3}\), \(h=2r\).
  12. Numbers \(20\) and \(20\), maximum product \(400\).

Answer Key — Test 4

  11. Optimal rectangle \(25\text{ m}\times25\text{ m}\), maximum area \(625\text{ m}^2\).
  12. Base side \(10\text{ cm}\), height \(5\text{ cm}\).

Answer Key — Test 5

  1. \(y'=3(8x-3)(4x^2-3x+1)^2\)
  2. \(y'=\dfrac{e^x(x^2+x+1)}{(x+1)^2}\)
  3. \(y'=\dfrac{2}{x(x^2+1)}-\dfrac{2\ln(x^2+1)}{x^3}\)
  4. \(y'=2\sec^2(2x-1)\)
  5. \(y'=e^{-2x}(\cos x-2\sin x)\)
  6. \(y'=\dfrac{2x+3}{2\sqrt{x^2+3x+2}}\)
  7. Local max \((-1,10)\), local min \((3,-22)\).
  8. Concave down on \(\left(-\dfrac1{\sqrt3},\dfrac1{\sqrt3}\right)\), concave up outside. Inflection points \(\left(\pm\dfrac1{\sqrt3},\dfrac{58}{9}\right)\).
  9. Decreasing on \((0,2)\cup(4,8)\), increasing on \((2,4)\). Local min \((2,10)\), local max \((4,14)\), inflection \((3,12)\).
  10. Local max \(\left(-\sqrt2,4\sqrt2\right)\), local min \(\left(\sqrt2,-4\sqrt2\right)\), inflection \((0,0)\).
  11. Dimensions \(20\text{ m}\times20\text{ m}\), minimum perimeter \(80\text{ m}\).
  12. Cut size \(x=4\text{ cm}\), maximum volume \(1024\text{ cm}^3\).
