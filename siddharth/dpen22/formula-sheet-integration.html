Integration Formula Sheet (DPEN22)

Core antiderivatives + methods map (u-sub, parts) + definite integral essentials.

1) Core Antiderivatives

\[ \int x^n\,dx=\frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1}+C\quad(n\ne-1),\qquad \int \frac{1}{x}\,dx=\ln|x|+C \]
\[ \int e^x\,dx=e^x+C,\qquad \int a^x\,dx=\frac{a^x}{\ln a}+C \]
\[ \int\sin x\,dx=-\cos x+C,\quad \int\cos x\,dx=\sin x+C \]
\[ \int\sec^2x\,dx=\tan x+C,\quad \int\csc^2x\,dx=-\cot x+C,\quad \int\sec x\tan x\,dx=\sec x+C \]

2) Substitution (u-sub) Pattern

\[ \int f(g(x))g'(x)\,dx = \int f(u)\,du \quad\text{with }u=g(x) \]
Trigger: inside-function + its derivative appears (or almost appears after constant factoring).

3) Integration by Parts

\[ \int u\,dv = uv-\int v\,du \]
\[ \text{LIATE guide for choosing }u:\ \text{Log}>\text{Inverse trig}>\text{Algebraic}>\text{Trig}>\text{Exponential} \]

4) Definite Integrals and FTC

\[ \int_a^b f(x)\,dx = F(b)-F(a)\quad\text{where }F'(x)=f(x) \]
\[ \frac{d}{dx}\left(\int_a^x f(t)\,dt\right)=f(x) \]
Sign meaning: definite integral gives signed area (below x-axis contributes negative).

5) Area Between Curves

\[ A=\int_a^b (\text{top} - \text{bottom})\,dx \]
SituationFormula
Vertical slices\(\int (\text{top}-\text{bottom})\,dx\)
Horizontal slices\(\int (\text{right}-\text{left})\,dy\)
