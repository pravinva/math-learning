Answer Key — Test 1

  1. \(\int (6x^2-8x+3)\,dx=2x^3-4x^2+3x+C\).
  2. \(\int (2x-1)^5\,dx=\dfrac{(2x-1)^6}{12}+C\).
  3. \(\int xe^x\,dx=e^x(x-1)+C\).
  4. \(\int \dfrac{3x+2}{x-4}\,dx=3x+14\ln|x-4|+C\).
  5. \(\int_0^2 (5x^2+1)\,dx=\dfrac{46}{3}\).
  6. \(\int_0^{\pi/3}\sin(3x)\,dx=\dfrac{2}{3}\).
  7. \(\int x\ln x\,dx=\dfrac{x^2}{2}\ln x-\dfrac{x^2}{4}+C\).
  8. Q8: \(A=\int_0^2(2x-x^2)\,dx=\left[x^2-\dfrac{x^3}{3}\right]_0^2=\dfrac{4}{3}\text{ units}^2\).
  9. Q9: Displacement \(=\int_0^5 v(t)\,dt=20\).
    Total distance \(=\int_0^1 v\,dt-\int_1^3 v\,dt+\int_3^5 v\,dt=4+4+20=28\).
  10. Q10: \(C'(q)=3q^2-18q+24\Rightarrow C(q)=q^3-9q^2+24q+K\).
    \(C(0)=40\Rightarrow K=40\). So \(C(q)=q^3-9q^2+24q+40,\; C(4)=56\).
  11. Q11: Total water \(=\int_0^{10}(2t+1)\,dt=110\text{ L}\).
    Average rate \(=\dfrac{110}{10}=11\text{ L/min}\).
  12. Q12: \(12-x^2=0\Rightarrow x=\pm2\sqrt3\).
    Area \(=\int_{-2\sqrt3}^{2\sqrt3}(12-x^2)\,dx=32\sqrt3\text{ units}^2\).

Answer Key — Test 2

  1. \(\int \left(15x^2+\dfrac{4}{x^2}\right)\,dx=5x^3-\dfrac{4}{x}+C\).
  2. \(\int 4(2x-3)(x^2-3x+1)^3\,dx=(x^2-3x+1)^4+C\).
  3. \(\int \dfrac{-8x}{(x^2-2)^2}\,dx=\dfrac{4}{x^2-2}+C\).
  4. \(\int e^{2x}(2\cos x-\sin x)\,dx=e^{2x}\cos x+C\).
  5. \(\int \dfrac{1-\ln x}{x^2}\,dx=\dfrac{\ln x}{x}+C\).
  6. \(\int 4\sin(4x)\cos(4x)\,dx=\dfrac12\sin^2(4x)+C\).
  7. \(\int_0^6|x-3|\,dx=9\).
  8. Q8: \(A=\int_{-1}^{2}(x+2-x^2)\,dx=\dfrac{9}{2}\text{ units}^2\).
  9. Q9: \(a=6t-4\Rightarrow v=3t^2-4t+C\). With \(v(0)=3\), \(v=3t^2-4t+3\).
    Displacement \(=\int_0^4 v\,dt=44\).
  10. Q10: \(P'(q)=-3q^2+24q-36\Rightarrow P(q)=-q^3+12q^2-36q+K\).
    \(P(0)=20\Rightarrow K=20\). So \(P(6)=20\).
  11. Q11: \(f_{\text{avg}}=\dfrac{1}{2-(-1)}\int_{-1}^{2}(x^3-3x)\,dx=-\dfrac14\).
  12. Q12: \(\int_0^3(kx+2)\,dx=\dfrac{9k}{2}+6=24\Rightarrow k=4\).

Answer Key — Test 3

  1. \(\int (6x^2+4x-5)\,dx=2x^3+2x^2-5x+C\).
  2. \(\int (2x-3)(x^2-3x+1)^4\,dx=\dfrac{(x^2-3x+1)^5}{5}+C\).
  3. \(\int \dfrac{2x}{x^2+1}\,dx=\ln(x^2+1)+C\).
  4. \(\int_1^3(3x^2-2x+5)\,dx=28\).
  5. \(\int \cos(5x)\,dx=\dfrac{1}{5}\sin(5x)+C\).
  6. \(\int xe^{-x}\,dx=-(x+1)e^{-x}+C\).
  7. \(\int_0^4|x-2|\,dx=4\).
  8. Q8: Area between \(y=x\) and \(y=x^3\) on \([-1,1]\):
    \(A=2\int_0^1(x-x^3)\,dx=\dfrac12\text{ units}^2\).
  9. Q9: Displacement \(=\int_0^5(t^3-12t+20)\,dt=\dfrac{425}{4}\).
    Average velocity \(=\dfrac{1}{5}\cdot\dfrac{425}{4}=\dfrac{85}{4}\).
  10. Q10: \(C(x)=\int (x^3-6x^2+9x+15)\,dx=\dfrac{x^4}{4}-2x^3+\dfrac{9x^2}{2}+15x+K\).
    \(C(0)=50\Rightarrow K=50\). So \(C(3)=\dfrac{407}{4}\).
  11. Q11: Work \(W=\int_0^5(x^2+2x+3)\,dx=\dfrac{245}{3}\text{ J}\).
  12. Q12: \(\int_0^{e-1}\dfrac{1}{x+1}\,dx=[\ln(x+1)]_0^{e-1}=1\).

Answer Key — Test 4

  1. \(\int (9x^2-18x+12)\,dx=3x^3-9x^2+12x+C\).
  2. \(\int (5x-2)^{-4}\,dx=-\dfrac{1}{15(5x-2)^3}+C\).
  3. \(\int \ln x\,dx=x\ln x-x+C\).
  4. \(\int_0^2 e^{3x+1}\,dx=\dfrac{e^7-e}{3}\).
  5. \(\int \cot x\,dx=\ln|\sin x|+C\).
  6. \(\int \dfrac{2x}{(x^2+1)^2}\,dx=-\dfrac{1}{x^2+1}+C\).
  7. \(\int_0^3(x^2-4x+3)\,dx=0\) (signed area cancels).
  8. Q8: \(A=\int_0^2[(4x-x^2)-x^2]\,dx=\int_0^2(4x-2x^2)\,dx=\dfrac{8}{3}\text{ units}^2\).
  9. Q9: \(R'(p)=3p^2-18p+24\Rightarrow R(p)=p^3-9p^2+24p+K\).
    \(R(0)=0\Rightarrow K=0\). So \(R(4)=16\).
  10. Q10: Displacement \(=\int_0^4(2t^3-9t^2+12t+1)\,dt=36\).
    Average velocity \(=36/4=9\).
  11. Q11: \(V=\int_0^{10}x(10-x)\,dx=\dfrac{500}{3}\text{ m}^3\).
  12. Q12: \(\int_0^{10}5e^{-0.2t}\,dt=25(1-e^{-2})\text{ L}\).

Answer Key — Test 5

  1. \(\int (12x^2-6x+1)\,dx=4x^3-3x^2+x+C\).
  2. \(\int \tan(2x-1)\,dx=-\dfrac12\ln|\cos(2x-1)|+C\).
  3. \(\int \sec^2(2x-1)\,dx=\dfrac12\tan(2x-1)+C\).
  4. \(\int \dfrac{2x+3}{x^2+3x+2}\,dx=\ln|x^2+3x+2|+C\).
  5. \(\int_0^3(x^3-3x^2-9x+5)\,dx=-\dfrac{129}{4}\).
  6. \(\int_0^2(x^4-2x^2+7)\,dx=\dfrac{226}{15}\).
  7. \(\int xe^x\,dx=e^x(x-1)+C\).
  8. Q8: Area between \(y=x^3-6x\) and \(x\)-axis on \([0,3]\):
    Split at \(x=\sqrt6\). Total area \(=\dfrac{45}{4}\text{ units}^2\).
  9. Q9: \(C'(t)=-t^3+9t^2-24t+30\Rightarrow C(t)=-\dfrac{t^4}{4}+3t^3-12t^2+30t+K\).
    \(C(0)=5\Rightarrow K=5\). So \(C(3)=\dfrac{191}{4}\).
  10. Q10: \(f_{\text{avg}}=\dfrac{1}{6}\int_{-3}^{3}(x^3-6x)\,dx=0\).
  11. Q11: Work \(W=\int_{10}^{20}\dfrac{400}{x^2}\,dx=20\text{ J}\).
  12. Q12: \(\int_0^4(kx^2+2x)\,dx=\dfrac{64k}{3}+16=40\Rightarrow k=\dfrac{9}{8}\).
