DPEN22 Differentiation Exam Pack — Questions

Five tests, 12 questions each. Includes stationary points, concavity, and full optimisation/curve word problems.
Excluded: implicit differentiation, parametric differentiation, and higher-order derivative drills.
Timing: 55-60 minutes per test.

Test 1

  1. Differentiate \(f(x)=3x^4-5x^2+7x-9\).
  2. Differentiate \(y=(2x-1)^5\).
  3. Differentiate \(y=(x^2+1)e^x\).
  4. Differentiate \(y=\dfrac{3x+2}{x-4}\).
  5. Differentiate \(y=\ln(5x^2+1)\).
  6. Differentiate \(y=\sin(3x)e^{-x}\).
  7. For \(f(x)=x^3-6x^2+9x+1\), find stationary points and classify them.
  8. For \(g(x)=x^4-4x^3+2\), find intervals of concavity and all inflection points.
  9. A production cost model is \(C(q)=q^3-9q^2+24q+40\), \(q\ge 0\). Determine where cost is increasing/decreasing and find local turning points with coordinates.
  10. The vertical position of an automated lift is \(h(t)=-t^3+6t^2-9t+5\), \(0\le t\le 5\). Determine when the lift rises/falls, find stationary points, and where concavity changes.
  11. A rectangular enclosure is built against a wall, so only three sides need fencing. If 200 m of fencing is available, find dimensions that maximize area and state maximum area.
  12. An open-top box is formed by cutting squares of side \(x\) from each corner of a \(30\text{ cm}\times20\text{ cm}\) sheet and folding up the sides. Find \(x\) maximizing volume and the maximum volume.

Test 2

  1. Differentiate \(f(x)=5x^3-\dfrac{4}{x}+6\).
  2. Differentiate \(y=(x^2-3x+1)^4\).
  3. Differentiate \(y=\dfrac{x^2+2}{x^2-2}\).
  4. Differentiate \(y=e^{2x}\cos x\).
  5. Differentiate \(y=\dfrac{\ln x}{x}\).
  6. Differentiate \(y=\sin^2(4x)\).
  7. For \(f(x)=2x^3-15x^2+36x\), find and classify stationary points.
  8. For \(g(x)=x^3-3x^2-24x+5\), find concavity intervals and inflection point.
  9. An arch profile is \(y=x^3-3x^2-9x+27\) on \([-2,6]\). Find turning points, classify them, and identify concavity changes.
  10. Profit is \(P(q)=-q^3+12q^2-36q+20\), \(0\le q\le 10\). Find increasing/decreasing intervals, turning points, and inflection behavior.
  11. A closed cylindrical container must hold \(500\text{ cm}^3\). Find radius and height minimizing surface area.
  12. Two non-negative numbers sum to 40. Model and solve for numbers maximizing product.

Test 3

  1. Differentiate \(y=(x^2+4)^3\).
  2. Differentiate \(y=e^{-x}(x^2-1)\).
  3. Differentiate \(y=\dfrac{2x+1}{x^2+1}\).
  4. Differentiate \(y=\ln(3x^2-2x+5)\).
  5. Differentiate \(y=\cos(5x^2)\).
  6. Differentiate \(y=x\ln x\).
  7. For \(f(x)=x^4-8x^2+3\), find and classify all stationary points.
  8. For \(g(x)=x^3+3x^2-24x+1\), determine concavity intervals and inflection point.
  9. Temperature is \(T(t)=t^3-12t+20\), \(0\le t\le 5\). Find where \(T\) rises/falls, identify stationary points, and discuss concavity.
  10. Flow rate is \(F(x)=x^3-6x^2+9x+15\) on \([0,5]\). Perform turning-point and concavity analysis.
  11. A farmer has 120 m of fencing to form a rectangle. Find dimensions maximizing area and state maximum area.
  12. An open-top tank with square base must have volume \(256\text{ m}^3\). If base side is \(x\), find \(x\) and height \(h\) minimizing material used.

Test 4

  1. Differentiate \(y=(x^3-1)^2\).
  2. Differentiate \(y=\dfrac{\sin x}{x}\).
  3. Differentiate \(y=(x^2+1)\ln x\).
  4. Differentiate \(y=e^{3x+1}\).
  5. Differentiate \(y=\ln(\sin x)\).
  6. Differentiate \(y=(5x-2)^{-3}\).
  7. For \(f(x)=-x^3+6x^2-9x+4\), find and classify stationary points.
  8. For \(g(x)=x^4-4x^3+6x^2\), determine concavity and whether inflection points exist.
  9. Revenue is \(R(p)=p^3-9p^2+24p\), \(0\le p\le 8\). Determine intervals, turning points, and inflection behavior.
  10. A particle path is \(s(t)=2t^3-9t^2+12t+1\), \(0\le t\le 4\). Find motion intervals, turning points, and concavity transition time.
  11. A rectangle has fixed perimeter 100 m. Determine dimensions maximizing area and the maximum area.
  12. An open box with square base and no lid must hold \(500\text{ cm}^3\). Find base side and height minimizing surface area.

Test 5

  1. Differentiate \(y=(4x^2-3x+1)^3\).
  2. Differentiate \(y=\dfrac{xe^x}{x+1}\).
  3. Differentiate \(y=\dfrac{\ln(x^2+1)}{x^2}\).
  4. Differentiate \(y=\tan(2x-1)\).
  5. Differentiate \(y=e^{-2x}\sin x\).
  6. Differentiate \(y=\sqrt{x^2+3x+2}\).
  7. For \(f(x)=x^3-3x^2-9x+5\), find and classify stationary points.
  8. For \(g(x)=x^4-2x^2+7\), determine concavity intervals and inflection points.
  9. Concentration is \(C(t)=-t^3+9t^2-24t+30\), \(0\le t\le 8\). Find intervals, stationary points, and inflection time.
  10. A beam deflection shape is \(y(x)=x^3-6x\), \(-3\le x\le 3\). Identify turning points, classify them, and determine concavity and inflection.
  11. A rectangle has fixed area \(400\text{ m}^2\). Find dimensions minimizing perimeter and state minimum perimeter.
  12. An open-top box is made by cutting equal squares from each corner of a \(24\text{ cm}\times24\text{ cm}\) sheet. Find cut size maximizing volume and the maximum volume.
