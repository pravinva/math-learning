Limits Formula Sheet (DPEN22)

Core patterns + trigger-to-method guide for weak-start students.

1) Limit Laws

\[ \lim_{x\to a}[f(x)\pm g(x)] = \lim f(x)\pm\lim g(x) \]
\[ \lim_{x\to a}[f(x)g(x)] = \left(\lim f(x)\right)\left(\lim g(x)\right) \]
\[ \lim_{x\to a}\frac{f(x)}{g(x)}=\frac{\lim f(x)}{\lim g(x)}\quad(\lim g(x)\neq0) \]

2) High-Use Special Limits

\[ \lim_{x\to0}\frac{\sin x}{x}=1,\qquad \lim_{x\to0}\frac{1-\cos x}{x}=0,\qquad \lim_{x\to0}\frac{1-\cos x}{x^2}=\frac12 \]
\[ \lim_{x\to0}\frac{e^x-1}{x}=1,\qquad \lim_{x\to0}\frac{\ln(1+x)}{x}=1 \]

3) Indeterminate Forms and Actions

FormTypical first move
\(0/0\)Factor + cancel, rationalize, common denominator, or L'Hopital if allowed
\(\infty/\infty\)Divide by highest power or L'Hopital
\(\infty-\infty\)Combine into one fraction
\(0\cdot\infty\)Rewrite as ratio first

4) Limits at Infinity (Dominant-term idea)

\[ \lim_{x\to\infty}\frac{a_nx^n+\cdots}{b_mx^m+\cdots} = \begin{cases} 0,& n<m\\ a_n/b_m,& n=m\\ \pm\infty,& n>m \end{cases} \]
Intuition: at huge \(x\), only highest powers matter.

5) Continuity Conditions

\[ f\text{ continuous at }x=a\iff \lim_{x\to a^-}f(x)=\lim_{x\to a^+}f(x)=f(a) \]
