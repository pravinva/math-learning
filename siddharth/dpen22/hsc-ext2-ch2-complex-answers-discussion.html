Test 4 - Answers

  1. \((\cos\theta+i\sin\theta)^4=\cos4\theta+i\sin4\theta\)
  2. \(\cos4\theta=\cos^4\theta-6\cos^2\theta\sin^2\theta+\sin^4\theta,\ \sin4\theta=4\sin\theta\cos\theta(\cos^2\theta-\sin^2\theta)\)
  3. \(\cos\frac{5\pi}{12}=\frac{\sqrt6-\sqrt2}{4}\)
  4. \(\theta=\frac{\pi}{6},\frac{\pi}{2},\frac{5\pi}{6},\frac{7\pi}{6},\frac{3\pi}{2},\frac{11\pi}{6}\)
  5. \(\theta=\frac{\pi}{12},\frac{5\pi}{12},\frac{7\pi}{12},\frac{11\pi}{12},\frac{13\pi}{12},\frac{17\pi}{12},\frac{19\pi}{12},\frac{23\pi}{12}\)
  6. \(z+\frac1z=\text{cis}\,\theta+\text{cis}(-\theta)=2\cos\theta\)
  7. \((1+i)^{10}=32i\)
  8. \(z=2\text{cis}\left(\frac{\pi+2k\pi}{5}\right),\ k=0,\dots,4\)
  9. \(\sum_{k=0}^{5}\text{cis}\left(\frac{k\pi}{3}\right)=0\)
  10. Equal angular spacing \(2\pi/n\) on unit circle gives regular \(n\)-gon.
Discussion: Q4-Q5 are pure angle-interval handling; write the general solution first, then filter by domain.

Test 5 - Answers

  1. \(z=-1+i\pm2\sqrt{1-2i}\) (equivalent exact forms accepted)
  2. \(\Log(-1+i\sqrt3)=\ln2+i\frac{2\pi}{3}\)
  3. \(z=2^{1/8}\text{cis}\left(\frac{\pi/4+2k\pi}{4}\right),\ k=0,1,2,3\)
  4. \(|z-1|^2+|z+1|^2=10\Rightarrow x^2+y^2=4\) (circle radius 2)
  5. \(\left|\frac{z-1}{z+1}\right|=1\Rightarrow |z-1|=|z+1|\Rightarrow x=0\)
  6. Sixth roots are \(e^{ik\pi/3}\), product \(=e^{i\pi(0+1+\cdots+5)/3}=e^{i5\pi}=-1\)
  7. \(z^4+z^2+1=0\Rightarrow z^2=e^{\pm2\pi i/3}\Rightarrow z=e^{i\pi/3},e^{i2\pi/3},e^{i4\pi/3},e^{i5\pi/3}\)
  8. \(\Re(z^3)=\cos3\theta=4\cos^3\theta-3\cos\theta\)
  9. \(|z|=2\Rightarrow z=2e^{i\theta}\), then \(z+\frac{4}{z}=2e^{i\theta}+2e^{-i\theta}=4\cos\theta\in\mathbb{R}\): all \(z\) on \(|z|=2\)
  10. \(e^{(i\omega-a)t}=e^{-at}(\cos\omega t+i\sin\omega t)\Rightarrow s(t)=e^{-at}\cos(\omega t)\). \(a\): decay rate, \(\omega\): oscillation frequency.
Discussion: Test 5 is transition-to-advanced style. For partial-credit, clear setup and representation choice still scores strongly.
